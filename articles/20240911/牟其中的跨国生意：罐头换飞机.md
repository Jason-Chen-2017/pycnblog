**代码示例：**

```python
import heapq

def find_cheapest_path(transport_data):
    min_heap = []
    heapq.heappush(min_heap, (cost, destination))
    while min_heap:
        cost, destination = heapq.heappop(min_heap)
        print(f"Cheapest path to {destination}: {cost}")
```

**代码示例：**

```python
def understand国际贸易术语(terms):
    descriptions = []
    for term in terms:
        if term == "FOB":
            descriptions.append("The seller is responsible for delivering the goods to the port of shipment and bears the risk until the goods are loaded on the vessel.")
        elif term == "CIF":
            descriptions.append("The seller is responsible for delivering the goods to the port of destination and bears the risk and cost of insurance and transportation.")
        elif term == "DDP":
            descriptions.append("The seller is responsible for delivering the goods to the buyer's premises and bears all costs and risks involved.")
```
