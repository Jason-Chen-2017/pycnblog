Introduction
------------

Time series analysis is a crucial field for many applications, including finance, engineering, and healthcare. Boosting algorithms have emerged as a popular method for time series analysis due to their ability to effectively detect patterns and predict future trends. In this comparative study, we will compare the popular boosting algorithms, including CatBoost, XGBoost, and their variants, such as AdaBoost and Gradient Boosting.
