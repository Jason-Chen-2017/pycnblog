医疗保健领域是国家经济社会发展的重要领域，也是各国政府职责所在。在我国，医疗卫生领域是人民群众最关注的领域之一，历经几千年历史，已经成为衡量一个国家在全球化进程中的角色、地位、作用和影响的综合性指标。然而，随着人类对医疗卫生服务的不断提升，传统的医疗服务越来越依赖于人力物力、手术费用越来越高等现实问题，越来越多的人选择接受全方位或高度体面健康管理。因此，如何利用人工智能技术提升医疗科技创新，是加速医疗卫生行业发展的必要之举。 

医疗卫生行业包括：基础设施建设、诊疗技术开发、医学图像识别、设备制造、检测服务提供、定价策略调整、保障措施构建等。其中，医学图像识别技术发展迅速，如通过数字切片等技术，可以在极短的时间内进行医疗影像的准确分析并实现精准诊断。同时，新型药物发现、开发、分子技术的应用也促进了人工智能技术的发展。

# 2.基本概念术语说明
## 2.1 人工智能（Artificial Intelligence）
> Artificial intelligence (AI) is intelligence demonstrated by machines, unlike the natural intelligence displayed by humans and animals, which involves consciousness and emotional response.[1] It involves computers capable of understanding and acting on their own behalf without being explicitly programmed to do so.[2][3] Machines can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence such as learning or problem-solving,[4] but also tasks considered intellectual or logical in nature such as reasoning, decision-making, planning, and speech recognition[5]. A subset of AI called machine learning enables machines to improve performance through experience and algorithms without being explicitly programmed.[6] The field has become increasingly complex over time with new techniques emerging constantly.[7] Some researchers are examining how robotics, biology, psychology, and other areas could help develop advanced artificial intelligence systems.

人工智能（英文：Artificial Intelligence，缩写为AI），通常被描述为机器智能，属于人机交互范畴。它主要研究如何让机器具有人类的某些能力，使其能够完成智能化的任务，并且不需要受到特别的训练就能独立运行，甚至有可能扩展到人脑无法理解和解决的问题。

人工智能系统由感知器、计算模块、规则引擎等组成，是指计算机系统模仿人的思维、行为方式，并对外呈现出智能化功能的一种技术。目前已有的应用系统可分为五大类，即搜索引擎、聊天机器人、文字识别与处理、图像识别与分析、智能决策与控制。

1956 年，麻省理工学院的两位哲学家罗素和圣莫利克·亚当斯提出，“智能”可以被定义为：“指将机械的理性同人的感觉、思维、情感等各种能力相结合所形成的能力。”因此，人工智能研究就是要发展一种具有人类特征的机器，来代替人类的大脑进行智能化的活动。 

2016 年，谷歌首席执行官埃里克森·施密特宣布，Google Maps 是人工智能上最热门的产品，并表示，“我们正在加紧投入大量资金和工程，以建立一个强大的地图数据库。”这意味着人工智能将在地图数据分析领域发挥关键作用。

## 2.2 深度学习（Deep Learning）
> Deep learning is part of a broader family of machine learning methods based on artificial neural networks. Learning refers to an automatic process of adjusting weights associated with connections between neurons in a network so that the network learns to produce accurate outputs for given inputs in a supervised manner. In deep learning, multiple layers of interconnected neurons are used to solve complex problems.[1] This technique is particularly effective in image and speech recognition, recommendation systems, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning domains. Advances in computing power have led to breakthroughs in deep learning, making it possible to train neural networks using large datasets and running on graphics processing units (GPUs).[2][3] The rise of big data has further boosted the interest in deep learning because it allows training models on ever larger datasets than previously feasible. Currently, deep learning has been applied to many applications including healthcare, finance, security, industry, social media analytics, and autonomous vehicles.[4]

深度学习（英文：Deep Learning）是机器学习的一个分支，是人工神经网络模型学习的一种方法。深度学习，即多层神经网络（Neural Network）的多级堆叠，其特点是利用多层非线性组合的特征提取、分类、回归功能，逐步提升对输入数据的抽象表示能力，最终达到自动化学习的目的。

深度学习是指利用多层神经网络，对大量数据进行迭代训练，从而学习到复杂的函数映射关系。这一技术得到广泛应用于图像、语音识别、推荐系统、自然语言处理、强化学习等领域。

## 2.3 医学图像识别（Medical Image Recognition）
> Medical imaging recognition (MIR) refers to the automated analysis of medical images to identify and diagnose diseases, determine treatment plans, monitor patient progression, and assist radiologists during surgical procedures.[1] This technology uses computerized algorithms to extract relevant information from medical scans obtained through various diagnostic modalities such as X-rays, CT scan, mammograms, ultrasounds, and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI). MIR provides significant benefits compared to manual interpretation of medical records: faster diagnosis times, better accuracy, and improved cost effectiveness for clinicians and patients alike. Despite its importance, there remains a paucity of reliable and scalable MIR tools and methodologies available to practitioners.[2] However, recent advances in artificial intelligence technologies have made significant progress towards developing robust MIR systems that can handle high-dimensional input data and classify medical images accurately.[3] Research in MIR has grown rapidly in recent years due to technological advancements and increased availability of digital imaging platforms. Several companies and organizations have contributed significantly to the development of state-of-the-art MIR tools and technologies, including Google Health, Amazon Web Services (AWS), GE HealthCare, Microsoft Azure, IBM Watson, and Nvidia Jetson TX series products and services.
