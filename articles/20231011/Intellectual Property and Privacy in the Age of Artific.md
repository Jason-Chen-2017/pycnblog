# 1.背景介绍

:
近年来，随着人工智能(AI)技术的飞速发展，越来越多的创新性技术已经涌现出来。比如，AlphaGo、谷歌的AlphaStar、Facebook的AI Photo Editing等等。但是这些技术带来的直接经济价值如何界定呢？而且，随着AI的普及和应用范围的扩大，我们是否需要保护用户的隐私信息呢？在大数据时代，保护个人隐私已经成为企业必须面对的课题。因此，本文将会讨论一下当前人工智能技术对个人隐私保护的影响，以及如何通过技术手段降低AI技术对用户隐私的侵犯。

# 2.核心概念与联系
## 概念
- Intellectual Property (IP): 知识产权是指由创作者对作品进行创作过程中产生的法律权利和非法律权利。包括版权、商标、专利、域名所有权等。
- Personal Data: 个人数据一般指能够单独或者与其他数据结合识别特定自然人生活状况的信息，包括姓名、地址、出生日期、电话号码、信用卡号码、行踪轨迹、婚姻状况、家庭情况、教育背景、财产状况等。
- AI Model: 机器学习模型是一种基于数据集对输入数据的预测和决策过程，可以利用经验和模式发现数据中的关系并推导出规律，而不需要明确地告诉计算机应该怎么做。
- Artificial Intelligence (AI): 人工智能，又称人工智慧，英文缩写为AI，是指由机器所表现出来的智能性和人类感知智能的综合能力。通常，它属于计算技术范畴的一类技术，强调让机器模仿人的思维方式、语言理解、决策和行为，使其具有分析复杂问题、解决问题和自我改进能力。
- Influence Diagram: 影响图是一个网络图形表示法，用于描述两者之间的关系，其中节点表示人或组织，边表示他们之间各种关系，颜色或粗细表示关系的强度。
## 联系
1. Intellectual property rights can be a serious concern for organizations using artificial intelligence systems as they rely on accurate models to make predictions or decisions that may impact individuals’ lives. For example, without proper intellectual property protections, AI technologies might accidentally mislead people who have been exposed to its output and damage their reputation or financial standing.

2. GDPR regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which applies globally, protect users' privacy when collecting personal data from online services like Facebook, Google, Apple etc. These requirements require companies to provide notice to users before processing any personal data about them and also give users an easy way to opt out if desired. Companies need to follow strict security measures to safeguard sensitive information stored in databases by encrypting it and implementing secure access controls.

3. The National Institutes of Health is currently exploring ways to use machine learning algorithms for predictive modeling of human health outcomes while adhering to HIPAA guidelines. With this model in place, researchers hope to identify factors that contribute to illness and help improve patient care and outcomes. However, potential concerns arise with regard to whether these techniques are biased against certain demographics or are susceptible to discriminatory effects. In addition, it’s essential to maintain ethical principles in all aspects of medical research and development. 

4. Machine learning tools used for artificial intelligence often produce unfair results due to the large amount of training data available to learn from. To address this problem, companies should use appropriate methods to evaluate the accuracy of models, collect feedback from users, and regularly update them based on new data sources. Moreover, companies must carefully consider how to handle protected health information, such as demographic information collected through biometric sensors, because gathering and analyzing this type of data could violate patients' privacy and potentially cause harm.
