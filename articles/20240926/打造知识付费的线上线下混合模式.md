### Core Introduction

Knowledge payment, as a new business model, has rapidly risen in popularity globally in recent years. At its core, knowledge payment involves sharing valuable knowledge content with users in exchange for payment, thereby achieving knowledge sharing and value realization. With the development of Internet technology, the forms of knowledge payment have become increasingly diversified, ranging from online courses, e-books, and audio lectures to offline training classes and workshops.

The main forms of online knowledge payment include online education platforms, e-book platforms, and knowledge payment apps. In comparison, offline knowledge payment mainly depends on physical training centers, lectures, and seminars. These forms usually provide more interactive and real-time experiences, better satisfying users' needs for in-depth communication and interaction. The advantages of offline knowledge payment lie in its ability to offer more direct and vivid learning experiences, although it also faces limitations in terms of venues, time, and costs.

However, the global spread of the pandemic has greatly impacted the offline knowledge payment model. Offline training centers have been forced to close, and lectures and seminars have had to be converted to online formats. This has led to the rapid rise of online knowledge payment, which has become the main force in the knowledge payment market. At the same time, users' expectations for a blend of online and offline models have also increased, hoping to combine the advantages of both to gain a richer and more efficient learning experience.

#### 5.1 协同过滤算法

协同过滤算法是推荐系统中最常用的算法之一。它通过分析用户的历史行为和相似用户的行为来预测用户对某一项目的兴趣。

**公式**：
\[ 
R_{ui} = \frac{\sum_{j \in S_i} v_{uj} \cdot v_{ij}}{\sum_{j \in S_i} v_{uj}^2} 
\]
其中：
- \( R_{ui} \) 是用户 \( u \) 对项目 \( i \) 的预测评分。
- \( S_i \) 是与项目 \( i \) 相似的项目集合。
- \( v_{uj} \) 是用户 \( u \) 对项目 \( j \) 的评分。

**例子**：
假设用户 \( u \) 给项目 \( i \) 的预测评分是 \( 4.0 \)，那么我们可以根据项目 \( i \) 的相似项目 \( S_i \) 来计算预测评分。如果相似项目的评分分别为 \( 3.5, 4.0, 4.5 \)，则：
\[ 
R_{ui} = \frac{3.5 \cdot 4.0 + 4.0 \cdot 4.5 + 4.5 \cdot 4.0}{3.5^2 + 4.0^2 + 4.5^2} = \frac{17.0 + 18.0 + 18.0}{12.25 + 16.0 + 20.25} \approx 4.25 
\]

#### 5.2 逻辑回归模型

逻辑回归模型常用于用户行为预测。它可以预测用户是否会在未来对某一项目产生兴趣。

**公式**：
\[ 
\hat{P}(Y=1|X) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(\beta_0 + \beta_1 x_1 + \beta_2 x_2 + ... + \beta_n x_n)}} 
\]
其中：
- \( \hat{P}(Y=1|X) \) 是预测的概率。
- \( X \) 是特征向量。
- \( \beta_0, \beta_1, \beta_2, ..., \beta_n \) 是模型的参数。

**例子**：
假设我们有用户 \( u \) 的特征向量 \( X = (2, 5, 3) \)，模型的参数为 \( \beta_0 = 1, \beta_1 = 0.5, \beta_2 = 0.3, \beta_3 = 0.2 \)，则：
\[ 
\hat{P}(Y=1|X) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(1 + 0.5 \cdot 2 + 0.3 \cdot 5 + 0.2 \cdot 3)}} \approx 0.972 
\]
这意味着用户 \( u \) 很有可能对项目感兴趣。

#### 5.3 软件推荐系统中的相似度计算

在软件推荐系统中，我们可以通过计算用户和项目的相似度来推荐相关的软件。

**公式**：
\[ 
sim(u, v) = \frac{\sum_{i \in I} w_i \cdot r_{ui} \cdot r_{vi}}{\sqrt{\sum_{i \in I} w_i^2 \cdot (r_{ui})^2} \cdot \sqrt{\sum_{i \in I} w_i^2 \cdot (r_{vi})^2}} 
\]
其中：
- \( sim(u, v) \) 是用户 \( u \) 和项目 \( v \) 的相似度。
- \( w_i \) 是项目 \( i \) 的权重。
- \( r_{ui} \) 是用户 \( u \) 对项目 \( i \) 的评分。
- \( r_{vi} \) 是用户 \( v \) 对项目 \( i \) 的评分。

**例子**：

### 5.1 Collaborative Filtering Algorithm

Collaborative filtering is one of the most commonly used algorithms in recommendation systems. It predicts a user's interest in an item based on their historical behavior and the behavior of similar users.

**Formula**:
\[ 
R_{ui} = \frac{\sum_{j \in S_i} v_{uj} \cdot v_{ij}}{\sum_{j \in S_i} v_{uj}^2}

- \( S_i \) is the set of similar items for item \( i \).
- \( v_{uj} \) is the rating of user \( u \) for item \( j \).

\[

Logistic regression is commonly used for predicting user behavior. It predicts whether a user will be interested in an item in the future.

**Formula**:
\[

- \( \hat{P}(Y=1|X) \) is the predicted probability.
- \( X \) is the feature vector.

\[

    prediction = model.predict(user_features)
    return prediction
