#### 4.1 机器学习中的基本数学模型

在机器学习中，数学模型是核心组成部分，用于描述数据之间的关系，并指导算法的学习和预测。以下是一些常用的数学模型及其公式：

1. **线性回归（Linear Regression）**：

   - **模型公式**：
     $$ y = wx + b $$
   - **解释**：$y$ 是预测值，$w$ 是权重，$x$ 是特征，$b$ 是偏置。
   - **应用**：用于预测物理实体的环境参数，如温度、湿度等。

2. **逻辑回归（Logistic Regression）**：

   - **模型公式**：
     $$ P(y=1) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(wx + b)}} $$
   - **解释**：$P(y=1)$ 是目标变量为1的概率，$e$ 是自然对数的底数，$wx + b$ 是线性组合。
   - **应用**：用于分类问题，如物理实体状态的分类（正常/异常）。

3. **支持向量机（Support Vector Machine, SVM）**：

   - **模型公式**：
     $$ \min_w \frac{1}{2} ||w||^2 + C \sum_{i=1}^{n} \max(0, 1 - y_i (w \cdot x_i + b)) $$
   - **解释**：$w$ 是权重向量，$x_i$ 是特征向量，$y_i$ 是标签，$C$ 是惩罚参数。
   - **应用**：用于分类和回归问题，如物理实体状态的分类和预测。

4. **神经网络（Neural Networks）**：

   - **模型公式**：
     $$ a_{\text{hidden}} = \sigma(W_{\text{hidden}} x + b_{\text{hidden}}) $$
     $$ a_{\text{output}} = \sigma(W_{\text{output}} a_{\text{hidden}} + b_{\text{output}}) $$
   - **解释**：$a_{\text{hidden}}$ 是隐藏层的激活值，$a_{\text{output}}$ 是输出层的激活值，$\sigma$ 是激活函数，$W_{\text{hidden}}$ 和 $b_{\text{hidden}}$ 是隐藏层的权重和偏置，$W_{\text{output}}$ 和 $b_{\text{output}}$ 是输出层的权重和偏置。
   - **应用**：用于复杂物理实体的建模和预测，如智能制造过程中的质量控制。

#### 4.1 Basic Mathematical Models in Machine Learning

In machine learning, mathematical models are the core components that describe the relationships between data and guide the learning and prediction of algorithms. Here are some common mathematical models and their formulas:

1. **Linear Regression**:

   - **Model Formula**:
     $$ y = wx + b $$
   - **Explanation**: $y$ is the predicted value, $w$ is the weight, $x$ is the feature, and $b$ is the bias.
   - **Application**: Used for predicting environmental parameters of physical entities, such as temperature and humidity.

2. **Logistic Regression**:

   - **Model Formula**:
     $$ P(y=1) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-(wx + b)}} $$
   - **Explanation**: $P(y=1)$ is the probability of the target variable being 1, $e$ is the base of the natural logarithm, $wx + b$ is the linear combination.
   - **Application**: Used for classification problems, such as classifying the states of physical entities (normal/abnormal).

3. **Support Vector Machine (SVM)**:

   - **Model Formula**:
     $$ \min_w \frac{1}{2} ||w||^2 + C \sum_{i=1}^{n} \max(0, 1 - y_i (w \cdot x_i + b)) $$
   - **Explanation**: $w$ is the weight vector, $x_i$ is the feature vector, $y_i$ is the label, and $C$ is the penalty parameter.
   - **Application**: Used for both classification and regression problems, such as classifying the states of physical entities and predicting their states.

4. **Neural Networks**:

   - **Model Formula**:
     $$ a_{\text{hidden}} = \sigma(W_{\text{hidden}} x + b_{\text{hidden}}) $$
     $$ a_{\text{output}} = \sigma(W_{\text{output}} a_{\text{hidden}} + b_{\text{output}}) $$
   - **Explanation**: $a_{\text{hidden}}$ is the activation value of the hidden layer, $a_{\text{output}}$ is the activation value of the output layer, $\sigma$ is the activation function, $W_{\text{hidden}}$ and $b_{\text{hidden}}$ are the weights and biases of the hidden layer, and $W_{\text{output}}$ and $b_{\text{output}}$ are the weights and biases of the output layer.
   - **Application**: Used for modeling and predicting complex physical entities, such as quality control in smart manufacturing processes.
