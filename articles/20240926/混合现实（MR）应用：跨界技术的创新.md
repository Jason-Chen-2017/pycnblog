### 1. 背景介绍（Background Introduction）

**1.1 混合现实（MR）的定义与发展**

混合现实（Mixed Reality，简称MR）是一种结合了现实世界和虚拟世界的交互技术。它通过增强现实（AR）和虚拟现实（VR）的结合，使用户能够与现实世界中的物体和虚拟环境进行交互。MR技术的发展可以追溯到20世纪90年代，随着计算机图形学、传感器技术以及计算力的不断提升，MR技术逐渐成熟并应用于多个领域。

**1.2 混合现实技术的重要性**

在当今数字化转型的浪潮中，混合现实技术以其独特的交互体验和丰富的应用场景，成为科技创新的重要驱动力。MR技术不仅为用户提供沉浸式体验，还为企业带来新的业务模式和商业模式，从而推动各行各业的创新和发展。

**1.3 跨界技术在MR中的应用**

MR技术的成功应用离不开与其他领域技术的深度融合，如人工智能、大数据、物联网等。这些跨界技术为MR技术提供了强大的数据支持和智能分析能力，使得MR应用更加丰富和多样化。

### 1. Background Introduction

**1.1 Definition and Development of Mixed Reality (MR)**

Mixed Reality (MR) is an interactive technology that combines the real world with the virtual world. By integrating Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR), MR allows users to interact with objects in the real world and virtual environments. The development of MR technology can trace back to the 1990s, and with the continuous advancements in computer graphics, sensor technology, and computational power, MR technology has become increasingly mature and applied in various fields.

**1.2 Importance of Mixed Reality Technology**

In today's digital transformation wave, MR technology, with its unique interactive experiences and diverse application scenarios, has become a significant driving force for technological innovation. MR technology not only provides users with immersive experiences but also brings new business models and business models to enterprises, thus driving innovation and development in various industries.

**1.3 Application of Cross-Disciplinary Technologies in MR**

The success of MR technology applications relies heavily on its integration with technologies from other fields, such as artificial intelligence, big data, and the Internet of Things. These cross-disciplinary technologies provide MR technology with powerful data support and intelligent analysis capabilities, making MR applications more rich and diverse.
