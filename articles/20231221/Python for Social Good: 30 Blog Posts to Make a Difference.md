The series will also feature interviews with experts in the field, case studies, and practical examples.

The goal of this series is to inspire and educate readers on how they can use Python to make a positive impact on society. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, there is something for everyone in this series. So, let's dive in and explore how Python can be used for social good!
