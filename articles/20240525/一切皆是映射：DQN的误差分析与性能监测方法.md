Everything Is a Mapping: Error Analysis and Performance Monitoring of DQN

摘要：DQN是一种基于深度神经网络的强化学习方法，可以通过对比预测和真实的Q值来进行误差分析。我们对DQN的性能监测方法进行了深入研究，探讨了用于监测DQN性能的各种指标。我们的研究表明，DQN在训练过程中存在多种性能问题，如过拟合、探索不充分、奖励不稳定等。我们为解决这些问题提供了一些建议，同时提出了一种新的性能监测方法，即基于回归的DQN性能监测方法。这种方法可以更好地捕捉DQN在不同阶段的性能，并为进一步优化提供有力支持。

关键词：深度强化学习，DQN，误差分析，性能监测

Abstract: DQN is a reinforcement learning method based on deep neural networks, and its error analysis can be conducted by comparing the predicted and actual Q-values. In this paper, we perform a deep dive into the performance monitoring methods for DQN, exploring various metrics for monitoring DQN performance. Our study shows that DQN has a variety of performance issues during training, such as overfitting, insufficient exploration, and unstable rewards. We provide some suggestions to solve these problems and propose a new performance monitoring method, namely, the regression-based DQN performance monitoring method. This method can better capture DQN performance at different stages and provide strong support for further optimization.
