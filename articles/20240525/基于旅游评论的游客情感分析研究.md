## 4. 项目实践：代码实例和详细解释说明

在本节中，我们将通过一个具体的项目实例来详细解释如何使用TextBlob进行情感分析。我们将对一组旅游评论进行情感分析，并计算每个评论的情感分数。

### 4.1 项目实例

假设我们有一组旅游评论，如下所示：

```
reviews = [
    "The hotel is great, the service is excellent.",
    "The hotel is terrible, the service is poor.",
    "The hotel is good, the service is okay."
]
```

### 4.2 使用TextBlob进行情感分析

我们将使用TextBlob对上述评论进行情感分析，并计算每个评论的情感分数。以下是具体的代码实现：

```python
from textblob import TextBlob

reviews = [
    "The hotel is great, the service is excellent.",
    "The hotel is terrible, the service is poor.",
    "The hotel is good, the service is okay."
]

for review in reviews:
    blob = TextBlob(review)
    sentiment = blob.sentiment
    print(f"{review}: {sentiment}")
```
