# （摘要）Sequential prediction is an important problem in stock market analysis where the next value of a time series depends on its previous values. In this work, we present a recurrent neural network-based sequential prediction approach to predict the price movements of multiple financial instruments using historical prices as inputs. The proposed model captures long-term dependencies between consecutive time steps by implementing a bidirectional LSTM (BiLSTM) structure that processes past data and future predictions simultaneously. To improve the accuracy of the predicted values, we employ attention mechanisms that focus on relevant features from different contexts at each step. Experiments show that our method can achieve significant improvements over several baselines including classical methods like moving average and autoregressive integrated moving average models. Furthermore, we demonstrate the applicability of our model to real-world financial applications such as portfolio optimization, investment decision making, etc. We believe that this new type of sequential prediction technique could greatly advance the research and practice of stock market analysis by enabling accurate and effective pricing strategies for financial instruments.

# 翻译摘要
时序预测是股市分析中的一个重要问题，其后续值依赖于前面值的影响。在本文中，我们提出了一个基于循环神经网络（RNN）的顺序预测方法，用于通过历史价格作为输入预测多支金融工具的价格走势。所提出的模型利用双向长短记忆网络（BiLSTM）结构实现了对连续时间步长间依赖关系的捕获，同时处理过往数据及未来预测。为了提高预测精度，我们采用注意力机制来从不同上下文中选择相关特征，并根据每一步做出适当调整。实验结果表明，我们的方法相比传统的方法比如移动平均线和自回归移项平均线模型，可以获得显著的改进效果。此外，我们还展示了我们的模型的可应用性，例如投资组合优化、投资决策等。我们相信这种新的类型的时间序列预测技术能够有效推动股票市场研究和应用的发展，为金融工具的定价策略提供可靠而有效的解决方案。
