With the widespread availability of copying and sharing technologies, it is difficult to effectively protect the intellectual property rights of digital artworks. Another challenge is the verification of authenticity. Ensuring that a digital artwork has not been altered during its creation, transaction, or exhibition process is crucial. Lastly, the issue of scarcity and ownership certification also arises. Blockchain digital artwork certification addresses these challenges by recording the metadata, transaction history, and ownership information of digital artworks on a decentralized and immutable blockchain platform. This technology not only solves the problems of intellectual property protection and authenticity verification for digital artworks but also enables the automation of transactions and management through smart contracts, thereby enhancing the transparency and efficiency of the art market.
