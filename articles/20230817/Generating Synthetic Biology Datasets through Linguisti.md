# 1.简介
  

Synthetic biology is a rapidly developing field in the biotechnology industry that aims to develop living organisms by combining genetic information and computer simulations with engineering principles such as cell culture technology and metabolic engineering. However, building synthetic data sets requires expertise in both computational methods and molecular biology. Furthermore, generating large-scale realistic datasets for testing machine learning models requires knowledge of organism physiology, gene expression patterns, interactions between genes, and so on. This article focuses on techniques that can be used to generate synthetic datasets using linguistic constraints rather than physical or chemical conditions. Specifically, we present a methodology for automatically generating biological systems based on natural language descriptions. The system designs are synthesized from user-provided textual specifications. We discuss our approach and provide examples of how it can be applied to different applications including modelling microbial communities and predicting protein function. We also note some limitations of this approach and suggest future directions for research in this area. Finally, we conclude with an outlook of where this work might take us in the near future.

# 2.相关背景
生成生物化学数据集是一个庞大的工程任务。如今，构建生物系统的模型在科研领域占据了越来越重要的地位。生物信息学、生命科学、分子生物学、计算机科学等领域的博士、硕士、博士后都可以参与到这一工作中。然而，构建真实数据集还需要大量专业知识和复杂的实验方法。在这个过程中，对细胞分裂、基因表达模式、蛋白质相互作用等特征进行仔细观察是非常关键的。因此，如何通过语言描述自动生成生物系统的数据集就显得尤其重要。近年来，基于深度学习的方法已经取得了一定的成功。但是，它们依赖于大量的训练数据，耗费时间长、成本高，而且仍然存在很多限制。为了减少数据收集成本和提升性能，一些研究试图利用人类创造性的思维和语言来制作生物系统。本文将会展示一种基于语言建模的生物分子数据集合成方法。用户提供的文本描述将用于合成生物系统。本文将讨论我们的方法及其应用范围，并举例说明如何应用它来模拟微生物群落以及预测蛋白质功能。最后，我们会谈论当前工作的局限性，并给出未来的研究方向。
