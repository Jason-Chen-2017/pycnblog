# 2.职场竞争力的定义、基本概念、以及相关术语
## 2.1 职场竞争力的定义
职场竞 competition ability
职场竞争力是衡量一个人的能力、品质和才能的一个标准。

一个成功的人士往往具有良好的职场素养、高度的责任心、沟通技巧、主动性、执行力以及能够克服困难和解决问题的能力。通过学习成长，可以让自己变得更加强大、更有竞争力。

职场竞争力并不是一劳永逸的，它随着时间的推移而变化，需要不断地学习和实践。因此，也不能单纯靠某一种能力或素质来衡量一个人的职场竞争力。

通过努力和不断地学习，个人可以将自己的职场竞争力提升到一个新的水平上去。

## 2.2 职场竞争力的基本概念
### 2.2.1 胜任力Competence(把握优势)
胜任力 is the strength and resilience necessary to perform well in a particular job or field of work. It represents one's proficiency at performing essential functions within a certain domain such as technical expertise, problem-solving abilities, attention to detail, time management skills, etc. Competence can be thought of as what an individual brings to the table when compared with others in the same occupation, industry, or organization. It can help individuals differentiate themselves from their competitors by establishing unique skills, knowledge, attitudes, values, and behaviors that make them stand out. Achieving competence requires hard work, dedication, and perseverance.

胜任力即掌握某项技能、知识、能力的程度。当一个人的技能达到要求时，他的胜任力就越高。胜任力通常包括技能、知识、感悟、洞察力、理解力等方面。

### 2.2.2 影响力Impact(维持组织形象)
影响力 impact is a measure of how much someone affects others by being compelling or persuasive in their behavior or ideas. Impact can come from factors such as word-of-mouth marketing, personal branding, reputation building, leadership skills, social media activity, etc. The more impact someone has, the greater its importance in society and therefore the better it stands out. In order for people to achieve high impact, they must have strong communication skills, inspiring leadership qualities, and drive to change the world. Impact can be measured through various performance indicators such as sales, influence, engagement, customer loyalty, etc.

影响力代表的是一个人的影响力大小。一个人的影响力越高，其在社会中的权威和声望也就越高。在职场上，个人的影响力主要体现在三个方面：第一，一个人的声誉；第二，其个人的魅力；第三，其所在领域的影响力。

影响力通常由外在因素驱动，如口碑营销、个人品牌塑造、名誉值建立、领导才能、社交媒体影响力等。获得高度影响力的个人必须具备极强的沟通技巧、激励性的领导才能、创造力和对社会进步的渴望。影响力可以通过目标设置、绩效评估、团队合作等方式衡量。

### 2.2.3 能动性Passion(不间断输出)
能动性 passion is the desire to continually develop new and innovative ideas, seek ways to improve existing products or services, or to overcome obstacles in pursuit of your goals. Passion helps individuals stay motivated and focused on their chosen goals, whether it be professional success, business growth, health improvement, or happiness. People who are highly passionate tend to be driven, self-motivated, persistent, enthusiastic, and impulsive. They thrive best when they have unlimited energy and focus, even in difficult situations.

能动性就是对某事物持续追求新鲜想法、寻找创意方法、打破困境、实现理想的热情。能动性对于保持工作热忱、学习进步、健康生活、幸福感都非常重要。能动性强的人往往最能持续精神的投入，并且往往被认为比躺着舒服的多。能动性强的人可以获得极大的乐趣、充满活力且永不满足的工作。

### 2.2.4 领导力Leadership(擢升领袖头衔)
领导力 leadership is the ability to inspire and empower others to reach their full potential. Leaders create a positive environment where team members feel valued, challenged, and supported. They know how to motivate, coach, and challenge team members, and enable them to grow and succeed. Leaders need to have a clear vision, effectively communicate the mission and objectives, demonstrate understanding of complex issues, and use collaboration and conflict resolution techniques to build a vibrant team culture.

领导力是指有领导才能的个人或团队成员，能够树立正面的品牌形象、激励员工、为团队带来成功。领导力不仅要求人有领导才能，还要求其具备领导风格、擢升领袖头衔、协调团队和解决冲突的能力。拥有领导力的人喜欢负责任务、热爱工作、乐于助人、适应复杂环境、勇于尝试。

### 2.2.5 意志力Willpower(坚韧不拔)
意志力 willpower refers to the power of a person’s inner grit to persist despite obstacles and failures. Willpower determines how successful an individual can be under pressure and pressures. While everyone wants to excel at something, some may struggle with either not having enough willpower or resisting the urge to push themselves. Some may lack confidence, others may be prone to panic attacks, while others may be able to handle setbacks with grace. Despite these challenges, those with a strong willpower are often the most powerful in completing tasks successfully.

意志力是指一个人内心的毅力。意志力决定了一个人是否能够胜任压力之下所需的工作。尽管每个人都想要得到超越，但有些人可能会因为没有足够的意志力或者厌恶挑战而无法取得成功。有的可能缺乏自信，有的可能会因恐惧而慌张，还有的人则能够克服重重困难，取得成功。

### 2.2.6 信任 Trust(树立信任关系)
信任 trust is the belief that someone or something you value deserves to be trusted. This level of trust is typically found among family and close friends, but also between coworkers and colleagues. Successful people have a deep sense of what is important and what isn't. When things go wrong, they don't blame others; instead, they speak up and apologize for mistakes and correct course to ensure safety for all involved parties. People with high levels of trust take pride in knowing they are doing good work.

信任是指对某人或事物的看法值得信赖。信任的层次通常在亲密的人和朋友之间存在，同时也存在同事之间的信任。一个成功的人有一个很深的自尊心，他们相信真诚、务实的重要。在糟糕的事情发生时，他们不会责怪别人，而是站出来说道歉、接受教训，确保所有相关方安全。拥有高级的信任的人会自豪地相信自己正在做好工作。

## 2.3 相关术语的简单描述
### 2.3.1 数字天赋 Numeracy
数字天赋 refers to the ability to manipulate numbers accurately and logically using basic mathematical concepts like addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and fractions. People with this talent can read and understand complex calculations quickly and apply them effectively to solve problems. Those with less numeral skills may struggle with arithmetic operations or with remembering long lists of numbers.

数字天赋是指能够准确无误地运用数学原理解决算术问题的能力。数字天赋的人阅读和理解计算过程，并可以有效地应用于日常生活的各种问题。而少数身处其中的人则可能由于缺乏数字天赋而遇到计算上的困难或记忆问题。

### 2.3.2 深厚的知识 Knowledge
Knowledge refers to both subject matter (e.g., biology, history, mathematics, psychology, sociology) and skill sets (e.g., problem-solving abilities, logical reasoning, decision-making, critical thinking). Good quality education provides individuals with extensive knowledge of subjects they study and utilizes these resources effectively to address day-to-day problems. Those without sufficient knowledge may encounter roadblocks or be unable to formulate adequate solutions.

知识分为两类：一类是对学科内容的熟练掌握（如生物、历史、数学、心理学、社会学），另一类是对技能的掌握（如解决问题的能力、逻辑推理、决策能力、批判性思维）。良好教育可以使个体拥有广泛的知识背景，并充分利用资源解决日常生活中的各种问题。而缺乏知识的个体则可能遇到瓶颈或无法正确构思解决方案。

### 2.3.3 耐力 Endurance
Endurance refers to the physical capacity of an individual to endure stresses and fatigue. Achieving high levels of endurance can help individuals maintain their mental, emotional, and physical health throughout life. Highly developed endurance enables individuals to perform tasks with extreme physical demands, working hours, or long periods of isolation. Those with low levels of endurance face challenges in coordinating daily activities, adapting to changing environments, and managing multiple priorities.

耐力是指一个人物对压力、疲劳、抑郁的耐受力。保持较高的耐力可以让人们在生命中保持精神、情绪、身体健康。拥有强大的耐力可以完成极端剧烈的身体活动、长时间的工作时间或孤独时间。而缺乏耐力的人则可能在日常事务上遇到障碍、适应环境的困难、处理多个任务的优先级问题。

### 2.3.4 思维潜力 Thinking Capacity
Thinking capacity refers to the intelligence, creativity, and originality of an individual. Experts with a high level of thinking capacity can think critically, analyze complex issues, and identify patterns. By breaking down barriers, identifying opportunity, and developing insights, experts can greatly advance their careers, live longer, and achieve success. However, if a person lacks this capability, he/she may struggle with making decisions or solving problems due to limited insight and judgment.

思维潜力是一个人具有的智力、创意和独特见解的能力。具有较高的思维潜力的人能够全面思考，严谨分析复杂的问题，识别模式。他们通过突破陷阱、发现机会、发现见解，可以极大地促进自身的职业发展，延长寿命，实现成功。然而，如果一个人缺乏这种能力，他/她可能因为缺乏见解和判断而导致无法做出正确的决策或解决问题。
