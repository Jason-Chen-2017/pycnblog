# 1.简介
  

## VR 是什么？
VR（虚拟现实）通常指的是增强现实技术，也就是通过计算机生成真实世界的环境，让用户在其中的三维空间中进行沉浸式的虚拟体验。VR的主要应用领域是娱乐、教育、科技、医疗等。随着VR设备的普及和更新，包括PC、手机、电视等都可以玩转虚拟现实。它的应用范围也越来越广泛。

## 为什么要做VR游戏或APP？
目前来说，虚拟现实已经成为人们生活的一部分。市面上有成百上千的VR游戏和应用，其中不乏那些让用户感到“如置身于现实之中”的玩法。基于这个趋势，VR也将会成为一个新兴行业。当今的消费者对高端的VR游戏和体验已经没有任何恐惧，以至于很多游戏公司都会把目光投向更高端的VR技术，尤其是VR硬件的更新换代带来的希望。


## VR开发者需要具备的知识背景
作为一个独立开发者或个人创作者，有以下必备的知识背景：

1. 对互联网相关基础知识有基本了解；
2. 了解图形学、物理引擎、声音引擎、脚本语言、C/C++等编程语言；
3. 熟悉常用的三维模型制作工具如Maya、3D Max、Blender等，并对相关的算法有一定的理解；
4. 有一定的项目管理能力，包括需求分析、设计文档编写、项目实施跟进等工作；
5. 具备较强的数学功底，包括线性代数、几何学、矩阵运算等方面的基础知识。

# 2.Virtual Reality(VR) Introduction and History
## 2.1 What is Virtual Reality (VR)?
Virtual Reality (VR), also known as Augmented Reality (AR), refers to a technology that generates an immersive environment using computer generated reality by placing virtual objects inside the user’s real-world environment. The main applications for VR are entertainment, education, science, medicine, and other fields. As VR devices become more popular and updated, including PCs, smartphones, televisions, etc., everyone can enjoy playing virtual reality games or experiences. The application scope of VR has grown dramatically over time.

## 2.2 Why do we need to develop VR games or apps?
The current state of virtual reality shows that it has become a part of people’s daily lives. There are many thousands of VR games and apps available on the market, some of which offer users a “realistic” experience. Based on this trend, VR will be a new industry. Today’s consumers don't have any fear about high-end VR games and experiences because they feel like they're actually being immersed into a real-world environment. Many companies look forward to higher end VR technologies due to the constant updates in VR hardware.

## 2.3 Knowledge background required for developing VR Applications
As an independent developer or individual creator, you should possess the following knowledge backgroud:

1. Basic understanding of internet related fundamentals;
2. Proficiency in graphics, physics engine, sound engine, scripting language, C / C ++ programming languages;
3. Familiarity with commonly used third-party modeling tools such as Maya, 3D Max, Blender, and an understanding of relevant algorithms;
4. Good project management skills, including requirement analysis, design documentation writing, follow-up of project implementation;
5. Strong mathematical foundation, including linear algebra, geometry, matrix calculation, etc., basic concepts.
