$$
\int_{a}^{b} \frac{d}{dx} f(x) dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

微积分的基本定理的证明：微积分的基本定理的证明是通过对积分和导数的定义进行推导得到的。具体证明过程如下：

$$
\frac{d}{dx} f(x) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x + \Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x}

$$
\int_{a}^{b} \frac{d}{dx} f(x) dx = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \int_{a}^{b} \frac{f(x + \Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} d\Delta x
$$

$$
\int_{a}^{b} \frac{d}{dx} f(x) dx = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \int_{a}^{b} \frac{f(x + \Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} d\Delta x = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} (F(b + \Delta x) - F(a + \Delta x)) - (F(b) - F(a))

$$
\int_{a}^{b} \frac{d}{dx} f(x) dx = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} (F(b + \Delta x) - F(a + \Delta x)) - (F(b) - F(a)) = F(b) - F(a)
