### 8. 附录：常见问题与解答

#### 8.1. What is the difference between symbolic AI, connectionist AI, and statistical AI?

- Symbolic AI focuses on rule-based systems, while connectionist AI uses neural networks, and statistical AI relies on probabilistic methods.

#### 8.2. How can we ensure that AGI will be beneficial to humanity?

- By developing explainable AI, focusing on AGI safety, and considering ethical considerations.

#### 8.3. Can quantum computing help accelerate AGI development?

- Yes, quantum computing has the potential to speed up certain types of computations, which could benefit AGI research. However, it also introduces new challenges related to hardware and software implementation.
