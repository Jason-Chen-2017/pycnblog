   $$ \min_{\vec{w}, b} \frac{1}{2} ||\vec{w}||^2 + C \sum_{i=1}^{n} \max(0, 1 - y_i (\vec{w} \cdot \vec{x}_i + b)) $$

   $$ P_j = \frac{e^{\alpha r_j}}{\sum_{i=1}^{m} e^{\alpha r_i}} $$
