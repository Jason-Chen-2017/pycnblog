\[ R(u, a) = f(t(u, a), d(u, a), t(a)) \]

函数\( f \)可以根据具体情况进行定义，例如：

\[ f(t(u, a), d(u, a), t(a)) = t(u, a) \times d(u, a) \times \frac{1}{t(a)} \]

\[ R(u, a) = t(u, a) \times d(u, a) \times \frac{1}{t(a)} \]
