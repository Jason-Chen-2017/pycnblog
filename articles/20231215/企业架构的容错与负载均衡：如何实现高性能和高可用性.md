FPR = \frac{Number\ of\ faults\ prevented}{Number\ of\ potential\ faults}

$$
TP = \frac{Number\ of\ requests\ processed}{Time\ interval}
$$

$$
SL = \frac{Number\ of\ requests\ processed}{Number\ of\ servers}
