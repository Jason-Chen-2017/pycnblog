## 8. 附录：常见问题与解答

### 8.1. what is numerical irreducible decomposition?

A numerical irreducible decomposition is a factorization of an algebraic variety into its irreducible components with multiplicities, computed using numerical methods.

### 8.2. what is tropical geometry?

Tropical geometry is the study of polyhedral geometries arising as limits of algebraic varieties over valuated fields.

### 8.3. how are numerical irreducible decomposition and tropical geometry related?

Numerical irreducible decomposition provides numerical approximations of algebraic varieties, while tropical geometry studies their combinatorial structures and degenerations. The two fields are connected via tropicalization, which maps algebraic varieties to tropical varieties by taking valuations of coordinates and operations.
