## 2.核心概念与联系
Google Cloud's sustainability initiatives are centered around three main pillars: carbon neutrality, renewable energy, and sustainable supply chain management. These initiatives are designed to minimize the company's environmental footprint and promote responsible resource usage across its operations and supply chain.

### 2.1 Carbon Neutrality
Google Cloud's commitment to carbon neutrality means that the company aims to offset all of its greenhouse gas emissions. This is achieved through a combination of energy efficiency measures, renewable energy procurement, and carbon offset projects.

### 2.2 Renewable Energy
Google Cloud is actively working to increase its use of renewable energy, which helps to reduce its reliance on fossil fuels and lower its carbon footprint. The company has set a goal to power all of its data centers with carbon-free energy by 2030.

### 2.3 Sustainable Supply Chain Management
Google Cloud recognizes that its supply chain has a significant impact on the environment. As such, the company is working to ensure that its suppliers adhere to strict environmental standards and are taking steps to reduce their own carbon footprints.
