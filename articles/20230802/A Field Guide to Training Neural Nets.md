# 1.简介
         
1943年，约翰·津巴布韦（John von Neumann）提出了著名的学习机理——感知机（perceptron），这在当时引起了极大的关注。然而直到上世纪五六十年代，才有真正意义上的神经网络模型问世，大规模的机器学习实践开始。这套基于反向传播（backpropagation）算法的深度学习系统已成为自然语言处理、计算机视觉等领域的标杆。本书将探讨神经网络的训练方法、核心算法和数学基础。希望能够帮助读者更好地理解、掌握和应用深度学习技术。
         # 2.阅读对象
         本书适合以下人员阅读：
         - 有一定机器学习基础的人；
         - 对深度学习领域感兴趣并对其发展趋势有浓厚兴趣的人；
         - 想要用深度学习解决实际问题的人。

         # A Brief History of Deep Learning and Where It Is Going: An Interdisciplinary Perspective on the Evolution of Artificial Intelligence
        In this book, we will explore how artificial intelligence has developed over time through a combination of history, theory, and application examples from across various fields. We will begin by examining some of the early achievements in computer science and machine learning that have led to deep learning's breakthroughs in speech recognition, image classification, natural language processing, and other applications. Next, we'll describe the current state of the field and its impact on society as well as further developments underway in related fields like robotics and autonomous vehicles. Finally, we'll look ahead at how deep learning is being applied to problems in healthcare, finance, social media, and many others, with potential impacts ranging from biosecurity to safety and security. By breaking down the complexities of AI development into historical, theoretical, and practical components, we hope to provide readers with a comprehensive understanding of the interconnected systems that are currently driving our world's most exciting technologies and future possibilities.
