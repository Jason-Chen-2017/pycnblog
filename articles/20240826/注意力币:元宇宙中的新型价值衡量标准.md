\[ A = f(U, I) = \alpha U + (1 - \alpha) I \]

\[ A = f(U, I) = \alpha \times 5 + (1 - \alpha) \times 4.5 = 5\alpha + 4.5 - 4.5\alpha = 0.5\alpha + 4.5 \]
