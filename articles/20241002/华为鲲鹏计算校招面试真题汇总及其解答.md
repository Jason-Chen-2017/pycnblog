### 核心算法原理 & 具体操作步骤

在解答华为鲲鹏计算校招面试真题时，算法问题通常是面试官重点关注的部分。以下我们将介绍几个核心算法的原理和具体操作步骤，帮助大家更好地应对面试。

#### 快速排序（Quick Sort）

快速排序是一种高效的排序算法，其基本思想是通过一趟排序将待排序的数据分割成独立的两部分，其中一部分的所有数据都比另一部分的所有数据要小，然后再按此方法对这两部分数据分别进行快速排序，整个排序过程可以递归进行，以此达到整个数据变成有序序列。

**具体操作步骤：**

1. 选择一个基准元素（通常选择第一个元素作为基准）。
2. 将所有比基准小的元素移动到基准的左侧，所有比基准大的元素移动到基准的右侧。
3. 递归地对左侧和右侧子序列进行快速排序。

**算法原理：**

快速排序的时间复杂度为O(nlogn)，在平均情况下表现优异，但在最坏情况下可能退化到O(n^2)。但是，通过随机化选择基准元素可以降低最坏情况发生的概率。

#### 动态规划（Dynamic Programming）

动态规划是一种用于求解优化问题的算法思想，其基本思想是将问题分解成子问题，通过子问题的最优解推导出原问题的最优解。

**具体操作步骤：**

1. 确定状态和状态转移方程。
2. 初始化边界条件。
3. 根据状态转移方程进行状态计算，通常使用二维数组存储状态。
4. 查找最优解。

**算法原理：**

动态规划的核心在于状态的优化和子问题的重叠，通过避免重复计算，提高算法的效率。

#### 红黑树（Red-Black Tree）

红黑树是一种自平衡的二叉查找树，用于实现关联数组等数据结构。其特点是每个节点都带有颜色属性，可以是红色或黑色。通过特定的插入和删除操作，红黑树能够保持平衡，确保查找、插入和删除操作的时间复杂度始终为O(logn)。

**具体操作步骤：**

1. 插入操作：
   - 将新节点作为红色节点插入到树中。
   - 对插入后的树进行一系列旋转和重新着色操作，使其重新平衡。

2. 删除操作：
   - 删除节点，将树中剩下的子树进行相应的旋转和重新着色操作。

**算法原理：**

红黑树通过特定的规则（如：每个节点至多有两个红色子节点、从根到叶子的每条路径上黑色节点的数量相同等）来保持树的平衡，确保查找、插入和删除操作的时间复杂度始终为O(logn)。

#### Core Algorithm Principles & Specific Operational Steps

When solving the recruitment interview questions from Huawei Kunpeng Computing, algorithm questions are often the focus of the interviewers. Here, we will introduce the principles and specific operational steps of several core algorithms to help you better prepare for the interview.

#### Quick Sort

Quick sort is an efficient sorting algorithm based on the idea of partitioning the input array into two parts, where all elements in one part are smaller than the other part. This process is recursively applied to the sub-arrays.

**Specific Operational Steps:**

1. Choose a pivot element (usually the first element as the pivot).
2. Move all elements smaller than the pivot to its left and all elements greater than the pivot to its right.
3. Recursively apply the quick sort to the sub-arrays on the left and right of the pivot.

**Algorithm Principle:**

The time complexity of quick sort is O(nlogn) in average cases, but it can degrade to O(n^2) in the worst case. However, randomizing the pivot selection can reduce the probability of the worst case.

#### Dynamic Programming

Dynamic Programming is an algorithmic technique for solving optimization problems by breaking down the problem into subproblems and using the optimal solutions of these subproblems to derive the optimal solution for the original problem.

**Specific Operational Steps:**

1. Determine the state and state transition equation.
2. Initialize the boundary conditions.
3. Compute the states based on the state transition equation, typically using a two-dimensional array to store states.
4. Find the optimal solution.

**Algorithm Principle:**

The core of dynamic programming lies in the optimization of states and the overlap of subproblems, which avoids redundant computations and improves the efficiency of the algorithm.

#### Red-Black Tree

A red-black tree is a self-balancing binary search tree used to implement data structures like associative arrays. Each node has a color attribute, which can be red or black. Through specific insertion and deletion operations, a red-black tree can maintain balance, ensuring that the time complexity for search, insertion, and deletion is always O(logn).

**Specific Operational Steps:**

1. Insertion:
   - Insert the new node as a red node into the tree.
   - Perform a series of rotations and recoloring operations on the tree after insertion to rebalance it.

2. Deletion:
   - Delete the node, and then perform the necessary rotations and recoloring operations on the remaining subtree.

**Algorithm Principle:**

A red-black tree maintains balance through specific rules (e.g., each node can have at most two red children, and the number of black nodes on any path from the root to a leaf is the same). This ensures that the time complexity for search, insertion, and deletion is always O(logn).

### 数学模型和公式 & 详细讲解 & 举例说明

在计算机科学和华为鲲鹏计算的领域中，数学模型和公式起着至关重要的作用。它们帮助我们理解和分析算法的复杂性、优化系统性能，以及解决实际问题。以下我们将详细介绍几个关键的数学模型和公式，并提供详细的讲解和举例说明。

#### 概率论与信息论

概率论和信息论是计算机科学中不可或缺的数学工具。它们在算法分析、数据压缩、通信系统等领域有广泛应用。

**概率分布函数（Probability Distribution Function, PDF）**

概率分布函数是描述随机变量取值的概率密度函数。例如，正态分布函数可以表示为：

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
$$

其中，$\mu$ 是均值，$\sigma^2$ 是方差。

**例子：** 假设一个随机变量 $X$ 服从正态分布 $N(\mu=100, \sigma^2=16)$，计算 $X$ 取值在 $90$ 到 $110$ 之间的概率。

$$
P(90 \leq X \leq 110) = \int_{90}^{110} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\cdot16}} e^{-\frac{(x-100)^2}{2\cdot16}} dx \approx 0.6827
$$

**信息熵（Entropy）**

信息熵是衡量随机变量不确定性的度量。在信息论中，信息熵可以表示为：

$$
H(X) = -\sum_{i} p(x_i) \log_2 p(x_i)
$$

其中，$p(x_i)$ 是随机变量 $X$ 取值 $x_i$ 的概率。

**例子：** 假设有两个随机变量 $X$ 和 $Y$，$X$ 的概率分布为 $P(X=0)=0.5, P(X=1)=0.5$，$Y$ 的概率分布为 $P(Y=0)=0.9, P(Y=1)=0.1$。计算 $X$ 和 $Y$ 的联合熵 $H(X,Y)$ 和条件熵 $H(Y|X)$。

$$
H(X) = -0.5 \log_2 0.5 - 0.5 \log_2 0.5 = 1
$$

$$
H(Y) = -0.9 \log_2 0.9 - 0.1 \log_2 0.1 \approx 1.51
$$

$$
H(Y|X) = H(X,Y) - H(X) = 1.51 - 1 = 0.51
$$

#### 矩阵与线性代数

矩阵和线性代数在计算机图形学、机器学习、计算机视觉等领域有广泛应用。

**矩阵乘法（Matrix Multiplication）**

矩阵乘法是一个重要的线性代数运算，其公式为：

$$
C = AB
$$

其中，$A$ 和 $B$ 是两个矩阵，$C$ 是它们的乘积。

**例子：** 计算以下两个矩阵的乘积：

$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 4
\end{bmatrix}, B = \begin{bmatrix}
5 & 6 \\
7 & 8
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
C = AB = \begin{bmatrix}
1\cdot5 + 2\cdot7 & 1\cdot6 + 2\cdot8 \\
3\cdot5 + 4\cdot7 & 3\cdot6 + 4\cdot8
\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}
19 & 26 \\
43 & 58
\end{bmatrix}
$$

**特征值与特征向量（Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors）**

特征值和特征向量是矩阵的一个重要概念，它们用于描述矩阵的性质。对于一个方阵 $A$，存在一个非零向量 $v$ 和一个标量 $\lambda$，使得：

$$
Av = \lambda v
$$

其中，$\lambda$ 是特征值，$v$ 是特征向量。

**例子：** 计算以下矩阵的特征值和特征向量：

$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
2 & 1 \\
-1 & 2
\end{bmatrix}
$$

通过求解特征方程：

$$
\det(A - \lambda I) = \begin{vmatrix}
2 - \lambda & 1 \\
-1 & 2 - \lambda
\end{vmatrix} = (\lambda - 3)(\lambda + 1) = 0
$$

得到特征值 $\lambda_1 = 3, \lambda_2 = -1$。

对于特征值 $\lambda_1 = 3$，求解方程组：

$$
(A - 3I)v_1 = \begin{bmatrix}
-1 & 1 \\
-1 & -1
\end{bmatrix}v_1 = 0
$$

得到特征向量 $v_1 = \begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
1
\end{bmatrix}$。

对于特征值 $\lambda_2 = -1$，求解方程组：

$$
(A + I)v_2 = \begin{bmatrix}
3 & 1 \\
-1 & 3
\end{bmatrix}v_2 = 0
$$

得到特征向量 $v_2 = \begin{bmatrix}
1 \\
-1
\end{bmatrix}$。

#### Probability Theory and Information Theory

Probability theory and information theory are indispensable mathematical tools in computer science. They are widely used in algorithm analysis, data compression, communication systems, and other areas.

**Probability Distribution Function (PDF)**

The probability distribution function describes the probability density function of a random variable. For example, the normal distribution function can be expressed as:

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
$$

where $\mu$ is the mean, and $\sigma^2$ is the variance.

**Example:** Suppose a random variable $X$ follows a normal distribution $N(\mu=100, \sigma^2=16)$. Calculate the probability that $X$ takes a value between $90$ and $110$.

$$
P(90 \leq X \leq 110) = \int_{90}^{110} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\cdot16}} e^{-\frac{(x-100)^2}{2\cdot16}} dx \approx 0.6827
$$

**Entropy**

Entropy is a measure of the uncertainty of a random variable. In information theory, entropy can be expressed as:

$$
H(X) = -\sum_{i} p(x_i) \log_2 p(x_i)
$$

where $p(x_i)$ is the probability of the random variable $X$ taking the value $x_i$.

**Example:** Suppose there are two random variables $X$ and $Y$, with $X$ having a probability distribution $P(X=0)=0.5, P(X=1)=0.5$, and $Y$ having a probability distribution $P(Y=0)=0.9, P(Y=1)=0.1$. Calculate the joint entropy $H(X,Y)$ and conditional entropy $H(Y|X)$.

$$
H(X) = -0.5 \log_2 0.5 - 0.5 \log_2 0.5 = 1
$$

$$
H(Y) = -0.9 \log_2 0.9 - 0.1 \log_2 0.1 \approx 1.51
$$

$$
H(Y|X) = H(X,Y) - H(X) = 1.51 - 1 = 0.51
$$

import numpy as np
import cv2
from PIL import Image

def compress_image(image_path, output_path, quality=75):
    """
    压缩图像
    :param image_path: 原图像路径
    :param output_path: 压缩后图像路径
    :param quality: 压缩质量（0-100），值越小压缩率越高
    """
    image = Image.open(image_path)
    image.save(output_path, quality=quality)

def edge_detection(image_path, output_path):
    """
    边缘检测
    :param image_path: 图像路径
    :param output_path: 边缘检测后图像路径
    """
    image = cv2.imread(image_path, cv2.IMREAD_GRAYSCALE)
    edges = cv2.Canny(image, 100, 200)
    cv2.imwrite(output_path, edges)

def scale_image(image_path, output_path, scale_factor):
    """
    图像缩放
    :param image_path: 原图像路径
    :param output_path: 缩放后图像路径
    :param scale_factor: 缩放比例
    """
    image = Image.open(image_path)
    scaled_image = image.resize((int(image.width * scale_factor), int(image.height * scale_factor)), Image.ANTIALIAS)
    scaled_image.save(output_path)

if __name__ == '__main__':
    input_image = 'input.jpg'
    output_image = 'output.jpg'

    # 压缩图像
    compress_image(input_image, output_image, quality=50)

    # 边缘检测
    edge_detection(input_image, 'edge_detection.jpg')

    # 图像缩放
    scale_image(input_image, 'scaled_image.jpg', scale_factor=0.5)

    """
    Compress the image
    :param image_path: Original image path
    :param output_path: Compressed image path
    :param quality: Compression quality (0-100), the lower the value, the higher the compression rate
    """

def edge_detection(image_path, output_path):
    """
    Detect edges in the image
    :param image_path: Image path
    :param output_path: Edge detection output image path
    """
    image = cv2.imread(image_path, cv2.IMREAD_GRAYSCALE)
    edges = cv2.Canny(image, 100, 200)
    cv2.imwrite(output_path, edges)

def scale_image(image_path, output_path, scale_factor):
    """
    Scale the image
    :param image_path: Original image path
    :param output_path: Scaled image path
    :param scale_factor: Scaling factor
    """
    image = Image.open(image_path)
    scaled_image = image.resize((int(image.width * scale_factor), int(image.height * scale_factor)), Image.ANTIALIAS)
