#### Definition and Classification of AI

Artificial intelligence refers to the simulation, expansion, and enhancement of human intelligence through computer programs and algorithms. AI can be broadly classified into two categories: Narrow AI and General AI. Narrow AI is specialized in specific tasks, such as voice recognition and image recognition, while General AI possesses a wide range of cognitive and learning abilities, capable of thinking and making decisions like humans.

#### Definition of Digital Infrastructure

Digital infrastructure refers to the foundational facilities that support the operation of digital technology and services. It includes hardware devices, network facilities, software systems, and data resources. The construction of digital infrastructure is crucial for enhancing social productivity and promoting economic development.

#### Relationship between AI and Digital Infrastructure

The relationship between AI and digital infrastructure can be seen as a mutually reinforcing and complementary one. 1. **Data Processing**: AI technologies are capable of analyzing and processing massive amounts of data, providing more precise and intelligent decision support for digital infrastructure.
2. **Automation**: AI technologies can automate the operation and management of digital infrastructure, reducing labor costs and improving operational efficiency.
3. **Resource Optimization**: AI technologies can help digital infrastructure achieve optimal resource allocation, improving resource utilization efficiency.
4. **Security Protection**: AI technologies play a crucial role in the security protection of digital infrastructure, including network attack defense and data security protection.
