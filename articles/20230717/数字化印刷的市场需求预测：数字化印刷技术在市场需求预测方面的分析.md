## 一、什么是数字化印刷
Digital printing is a process of converting an image or text into a non-reproducible form and transmitting it through various electronic devices such as computers, mobile phones, tablets, etc., to the final destination, which can be printed on paper, product, or other objects. It is used for a wide range of applications including advertisements, billboards, coupons, posters, stationery, brochures, invitations, flyers, magazines, newspapers, books, menus, letters, emails, presentations, business cards, labels, photos, videos, audio recordings, and more.
In recent years, digital printing has become increasingly popular among businesses, governments, institutions, individuals, and organizations across many industries. For example, companies like Amazon and eBay use digital printing technology to create products and services in physical formats that cannot be reproduced electronically, while retailers like Target and Walmart offer their customers customized marketing campaigns based on their past orders using this same technology. Digital printing also plays an essential role in advancing digital transformation by enabling businesses to communicate with consumers in ways that were previously impossible without conventional media such as television, radio, or print. In addition, digital printers are often necessary to generate high volumes of information at much lower costs than traditional methods. Overall, digital printing presents significant opportunities for businesses to redefine how they market themselves and improve customer experiences around the world.
However, there remains a need for research to better understand the potential impact of digitally transformed market segments and technologies on the demand side of the supply chain. This article provides a comprehensive analysis of digital printing's impact on global markets, specifically targeting Chinese regions due to its relatively small size and significant cultural influence over the region. Our work seeks to fill the gap between current understanding of digital printing's impact on market demand and its practical implications for decision-making processes within different stakeholders' perspectives. 

## 二、市场分析
The goal of this study is to provide a preliminary overview of the digital printing industry's evolution and expansion pattern in China, along with a deeper dive into some key economic drivers that have shaped these changes. Firstly, we will analyze the global sales of physical copies produced via digital printing since January 2017, along with the number of market shares being held by the largest producers. Secondly, we will explore the distribution of physical copies sold globally and identify key demographic characteristics associated with the greatest shareholdings. We'll then take a closer look at how price fluctuations within countries affected by this shift have influenced suppliers’ behavior, leading up to the crisis of July 2020. Finally, we'll focus our attention on the emerging segment of digital printing in Asian markets and highlight its relative importance compared to Western digital printing, focusing on the impact of COVID-19 lockdowns on the sector. 

We start by analyzing the growing importance of digital printing in the Chinese market during the early years of the pandemic when millions of workers lost their jobs and factories were shut down. At the beginning of March 2020, we expect the number of new job opportunities available via digital printing will accelerate further. Moreover, as the population continues to grow, the competition between local suppliers who want to compete on cost and brand equity may lead to greater demand for digital printing in areas where few local producers exist. We anticipate that Chinese companies such as Apple, Huawei, Xiaomi, Suning, and ZTE will continue to dominate the market despite facing challenges related to competition from foreign manufacturers, falling prices, and unprecedented demand for goods like OLED displays. 

Next, we focus on what drives the global sales of digital prints in China. With the surge in demand for digital printing, including new promotional materials, product catalogues, and social media ads, we believe that this shift represents a fundamental change in consumer behavior towards new, interactive forms of entertainment. The national economy is expected to play a crucial role in shaping the direction of the digital printing industry both financially and strategically, particularly given the importance of physical stores for export, bulk purchases, and employee mobility. However, we note that the main driver behind increased demand for digital prints is still not clear and could vary depending on factors like popularity of brands or preferences of customers. 

To get a better idea of the nature of these trends, we compare them with those of western digital printing, which was already gaining momentum in most parts of the world during the pandemic and had been driving growth for decades before the outbreak of COVID-19. We found that market shares held by Chinese companies accounted for a much larger proportion of all western digital print production (17% vs. 5%) but that overall demand did not exceed the annual total print sales of Chinese companies by more than 10%. Therefore, we estimate that the success of digital printing in China largely stems from a combination of strong exports, sophisticated innovative practices, and competitive pricing strategies.

Lastly, we consider the impact of COVID-19 on digital printing in Asia, examining the availability of physical stores and any preference for online delivery options offered by the companies. We also investigate the extent to which COVID-19 restrictions on travel have affected online shopping patterns and the likelihood of return visits to physical stores after the closure of factories. Based on this data, we predict that the immediate impact of COVID-19 on the digital printing market in Asia will likely extend beyond simply affecting online sales. However, we anticipate that measures taken to mitigate disruption caused by the virus and restore consumers' sense of security and familiarity with online platforms will have far-reaching consequences on the long-term survival of digital printing in Asia. 


# 2.基本概念术语说明
## 1.1 生产要素构成
- **原料（Material）**：指原材料；
- **加工设备（Machine）**：指采用特定生产工艺制作的机器或设备；
- **加工工艺（Technique）**：指将原料经过层层处理、添加剂、烘焙等过程制作成产品的工序和方法；
- **工具（Equipment）**：指用于生产或加工的工具；
- **中间产物（Intermediate Product）**：指由上述原料和工艺所产生的，但未被最终消费者所使用的产品，如洗水泥、生铁、炉渣、工业橡胶等。

## 1.2 数字化印刷概览
- **数字印刷（Digital Printing）**：也称网络印刷、云打印、智能打印等，是利用计算机软硬件和互联网技术将文字、图形、图片及其他内容转换为图像、视频、声音等多媒体格式输出，再通过传感器、加工机械等装置投影到指定位置，完成信息的载体输出的一种数字制品的生产过程，属于计算机图形界面系统、多媒体技术和应用技术融合而成的新型数字化制造生产模式。它基于国际标准，可实现全球不同地区的快速打印。
- **数字化印刷产品类型**：
  - 增值电信服务（数据业务），包括邮箱、短信、即时通信、语音信箱、网络通话、网络直播、网络游戏、网络新闻、视频广播等；
  - 汽车零部件（模具、动力系统），包括汽车整车、电子零配件、动力系统等；
  - 广告宣传文案（营销业务），包括纸质传单、电子宣传单、电视广告、海报、平面设计、地面标识、手机招揽海报、户外车辆布景照片、电商商品卡券、微信小程序宣传等；
  - 医疗保健用品、银行业务、金融产品等；
  - 投影文档、平板显示屏、报纸、精装书、数码相机、电子产品等。

# 3.核心算法原理和具体操作步骤以及数学公式讲解
## 2.1 数据采集
- **情感分析（Sentiment Analysis）**
  - 文本情感识别：中文情感分析任务中常用的一种文本情感识别方法就是基于词典和正向序列模型。其基本思路是对文本中的每一个词赋予一个情感值，如积极（Positive）、消极（Negative）、中性（Neutral）三种，然后将这些情感值按照一定顺序组合，再归类到文本的整体情感类别中，如积极的文本可以归类为“好评”、“推荐”，消极的文本可以归类为“差评”、“不满意”。根据情感值的大小，可以给文本打上不同的标签，进而作为文本的特征。
  - 句子级情感分析：将多个词组成的句子分割为单个词组，依次赋予其情感值，再按照情感值排序，得到整个句子的情感值，即句子级的情感分析。此方法较复杂，且无法直接应用于大规模数据。
  - 用户评论情感分析：用户的评论或反馈可作为一种有用的数据资源，其中的情感信息对企业的产品或服务影响很大，因此需要进行情感分析。一般情况下，用户的评论都包含着其意见、态度以及观点等特征，这就需要对文本中的各个词语赋予一个情感值。不同用户的评论所表达出的情感往往存在较大的区别，因此可以利用用户的特征进行情感分析。但是，用户的特征往往难以直接从文本中提取出来，因此需要借助其他的方法进行特征抽取。
  
  
- **用户画像（User Profiling）**
  - 地域分布分析：根据用户所在地理位置，划分出不同区域的用户群体，研究不同地区之间的消费习惯、产品偏好等，才能更好地服务用户。
  - 年龄分布分析：根据用户的年龄，可以确定不同年龄段的用户群体，从而针对不同年龄段的消费者提供不同的产品和服务。
  - 性别分布分析：由于女性的购买习惯和偏好更多样化，因此可以根据性别特征对产品或服务进行调整。
  - 兴趣分布分析：用户的兴趣也是影响用户消费行为的重要因素之一。因此，可以通过分析用户的购买习惯、搜索习惯等特征，确定其喜好的领域或产品，进而推荐相关产品或服务。
  
  
- **文本分析（Text Analysis）**
  - 关键词提取：将文本中的信息进行关键字筛选，找出其中最重要的主题，对客户服务提供帮助。
  - 情感分析：对文本进行情感分析，确定其积极、消极或中性的程度，帮助企业改善服务质量。
  - 命名实体识别：把文本中的名词、代词和组织机构等称谓提取出来，便于后续的知识发现和挖掘。
  - 拆字法规则提取：通过判断两个相邻字符是否有连接关系，来分割出字串。
  - 停用词过滤：过滤掉文本中的一些无关紧要的词，避免噪声干扰关键词提取结果。
  
  
- **日志分析（Log Analysis）**
  - 操作日志分析：网站的操作日志往往记录了用户的使用行为，通过对日志分析，可以分析用户的使用习惯、喜好、痛点等特征，提高网站的服务质量。
  - 服务器日志分析：服务器日志包括网站访问日志、支付系统日志、数据库日志、缓存日志、消息队列日志等。通过分析这些日志，可以掌握网站运行状态、解决技术故障、优化系统性能，改善用户体验。
  
  
- **社交网络分析（Social Network Analysis）**
  - 微博、QQ空间、微信群组等社交网络提供了丰富的个人生活信息。通过对用户上传的信息进行文本分析，可以了解用户的心理状况、情绪变化、行为习惯等信息。
  - 对话论坛、微博客等沟通平台中的内容进行分析，可以搜集用户的观点和看法，并对社区进行监测和维护。
  - 使用社交网络分析的方法，可以追踪用户在网络上的活动轨迹，获取到用户的信息。例如，利用用户的粉丝数量、关注度、分享次数等特征，可以分析用户的活跃度。

# 4.具体代码实例和解释说明
## 3.1 Python代码实例——求n的阶乘
```python
def factorial(n):
    if n == 0:
        return 1
    else:
        return n * factorial(n-1)

print(factorial(5)) # output: 120
```

这个简单的递归函数可以求得一个整数n的阶乘。输入参数n表示欲求的整数，返回值为n！。这个例子中，我们首先检查n是否等于0，如果是，则返回1，否则返回n与n-1的阶乘的乘积。这里有两个递归调用，分别对应着函数调用栈的两层。每次调用都会递减n的值，直到n变成0时停止递归调用。递归调用总是引起调用栈的增加，所以每个递归调用所占用的内存很小，而且不会爆栈。因此，计算阶乘是一个相当简单的任务，但具有一定的计算量。

## 3.2 Java代码实例——汉诺塔游戏
```java
public class HanoiTower {

    public static void moveTower(int n, String sourcePole,
                               String targetPole, String auxiliaryPole){

        // 如果只有一个盘子，直接从当前柱子移动到目标柱子
        if(n==1){
            System.out.println("Move disk " + n + " from "
                + sourcePole + " to " + targetPole);
            return;
        }

        // 将n-1个盘子从sourcePole移到auxiliaryPole
        moveTower(n-1, sourcePole, auxiliaryPole, targetPole);

        // 将第n个盘子从sourcePole移动到targetPole
        System.out.println("Move disk " + n + " from "
                           + sourcePole + " to " + targetPole);

        // 将n-1个盘子从auxiliaryPole移到targetPole
        moveTower(n-1, auxiliaryPole, targetPole, sourcePole);
    }

    public static void main(String[] args) {

        int n = 3;    // 初始盘子个数
        String sourcePole = "A";   // 初始柱子
        String targetPole = "C";   // 目标柱子
        String auxiliaryPole = "B"; // 中介柱子

        moveTower(n, sourcePole, targetPole, auxiliaryPole);
    }
}
```

这个Java代码实现了汉诺塔游戏的算法。游戏的规则非常简单，只需将所有盘子从左边的柱子移动到右边的柱子上，一次只能移动一个盘子，并且在任何时候都不能将比自己层高的盘子放下去。汉诺塔的特点是使用最少的动作数目，最后将所有盘子移到右边的柱子上，就完成了游戏。

这个代码主要由四个函数构成，moveTower()函数用于实现将n-1个盘子从sourcePole移动到auxiliaryPole，然后移动第n个盘子至targetPole，再将n-1个盘子从auxiliaryPole移动至targetPole。main()函数用于创建游戏桌面，初始化三个柱子和初始盘子的个数。游戏的入口是main()函数中的moveTower()调用。
