3. 熵 H(x) = -Σ P(x_i) * log2(P(x_i))
4. 信息熵 H(X) = -Σ P(x_i) * log2(P(x_i))
5. 互信息 I(X;Y) = H(X) - H(X|Y)
6. 条件熵 H(X|Y) = -Σ P(x_i,y_j) * log2(P(x_i|y_j))
7. 条件概率 P(A|B) = P(A∩B) / P(B)
8. 贝叶斯定理 P(A|B) = P(B|A) * P(A) / P(B)
