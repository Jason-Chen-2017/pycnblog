### 1.1 5G技术概述

5G（第五代移动通信技术）是当前通信技术的最新发展，与4G相比，5G在速度、延迟和连接密度等方面都有了显著提升。5G的主要特点包括：

- **高速率**：5G的理论下载速度可以达到10Gbps，是4G的百倍以上。
- **低延迟**：5G的端到端延迟可以降低到1毫秒，极大地提高了实时响应能力。
- **大连接**：5G能够支持数百万设备的连接，满足大规模物联网应用的接入需求。

### 1.2 物联网的发展

物联网是指通过互联网将各种设备连接起来，实现信息交换和通信的系统。随着传感技术、数据处理能力和通信技术的进步，物联网应用日益丰富，包括智能家居、智能城市、工业自动化等领域。

### 1.3 5G与物联网的结合

5G的高速低延迟特性为物联网应用提供了强大的基础设施支持。例如，在自动驾驶领域，5G可以实现车辆与周围环境的高速数据交换，确保驾驶决策的实时性和准确性。在远程医疗领域，5G可以提供稳定的低延迟连接，支持医生与患者之间的实时视频诊断和治疗。

## 1. Background Introduction
### 1.1 Overview of 5G Technology

5G, the fifth generation of mobile communication technology, represents the latest advancement in telecommunications. Compared to 4G, 5G offers significant improvements in speed, latency, and connection density. Key features of 5G include:

- **High Speed**: The theoretical download speed of 5G can reach up to 10Gbps, which is over a hundred times faster than 4G.
- **Low Latency**: The end-to-end latency of 5G can be reduced to as low as 1 millisecond, greatly enhancing real-time responsiveness.
- **Massive Connectivity**: 5G can support millions of device connections, meeting the requirements of large-scale IoT applications.

### 1.2 The Development of IoT

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the network of devices connected through the internet to exchange information and communicate. With advancements in sensor technology, data processing capabilities, and communication technology, IoT applications have become increasingly diverse, including smart homes, smart cities, and industrial automation.

### 1.3 The Integration of 5G and IoT

The high-speed and low-latency capabilities of 5G provide strong infrastructure support for IoT applications. For example, in the field of autonomous driving, 5G enables high-speed data exchange between vehicles and their surroundings, ensuring real-time and accurate driving decisions. In the field of telemedicine, 5G provides stable and low-latency connections to support real-time video diagnosis and treatment between doctors and patients.
