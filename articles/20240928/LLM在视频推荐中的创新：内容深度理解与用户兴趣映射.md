近年来，随着深度学习和自然语言处理技术的飞速发展，大规模语言模型（LLM，Large Language Model）在文本生成、翻译、问答等任务上取得了显著的突破。与此同时，视频推荐作为互联网领域的一个重要应用场景，也正面临着技术革新和挑战。传统的视频推荐系统主要依赖于用户行为数据和视频内容特征，如播放时长、浏览记录、视频标签等。然而，随着视频内容的多样性和复杂性不断增加，单纯依赖这些表面特征已难以满足用户对个性化推荐的需求。

### Abstract
This article delves into the innovative application of Large Language Models (LLMs) in video recommendation systems. With the rapid advancement in deep learning and natural language processing, LLMs have demonstrated remarkable success in text generation, translation, and question-answering tasks. However, traditional video recommendation systems, which rely heavily on user behavior data and video content features, struggle to meet the increasing demand for personalized recommendations. This paper explores the integration of LLMs in video recommendation, covering core algorithm principles, mathematical models, practical implementations, and real-world applications. By the end of this article, readers will gain a comprehensive understanding of LLM-based video recommendation systems and their potential future developments and challenges.
