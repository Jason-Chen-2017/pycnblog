   4000 - 20p & \text{if } p > 40

   \[ X = \begin{bmatrix}

   x_{m1} & x_{m2} & \cdots & x_{mn}

   \[ S = \begin{bmatrix}
   s_{11} & s_{12} & \cdots & s_{1n} \\

   \[ R = S \cdot X^T \]
