# 1.简介
  

Data quality is one of the most critical issues in any organization dealing with data. It refers to the accuracy, completeness, consistency and reliability of the data collected from various sources such as databases, sensors, web pages etc., to ensure that it meets the requirements specified by users or other stakeholders of the organization. Data quality plays a significant role in decision-making processes for organizations, particularly those involved in business intelligence, marketing analysis, supply chain management, e-commerce etc., where accurate and reliable information is essential for making critical decisions. However, achieving high-quality data has become an increasingly complex task due to the enormous amount of raw data generated every day. As such, there is a need to identify appropriate techniques and tools to assist businesses in managing their data effectively. In this article, we will explore some fundamental concepts and principles related to data quality, discuss about core algorithms and methods used to assess data quality, provide practical examples using industry standard tools and finally outline future challenges and directions for advancement.
