1. 前向传播：$$
\text{output} = \text{activation}(\text{input} \cdot \text{weights} + \text{bias})
$$

2. 反向传播：$$
\text{gradient} = \frac{\partial \text{loss}}{\partial \text{weights}}
$$

3. 损失函数：$$
\text{loss} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} (\text{target} - \text{output})^2
