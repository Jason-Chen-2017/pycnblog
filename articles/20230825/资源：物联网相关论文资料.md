# 1.简介
  

物联网(Internet of Things, IoT)由传感器、控制器、处理器等组件组成，通过互联网与其它设备连接，实现数据收集、处理、传输及展示。其目的是为了解决目前信息化建设中存在的单点故障、弱覆盖、低速度、高时延等问题，提升终端产品的可靠性、普适性、智能化程度。同时通过互联网云服务平台、大数据分析手段、应用生态系统提供商、移动支付、智慧城市、可穿戴设备、机器人与无人机等新型应用领域。本文将以供需服务（即需要数据、服务及计算能力的用户）为例，整合2017年国内外物联网相关论文，进行论述并给出典型的案例研究。

# 3.论文及讲座
## [1] Wireless Sensor Network for Industrial Monitoring and Control based on LPWAN Technologies
**Abstract:** Wireless sensor networks (WSNs) have emerged as a promising technology in the last years due to their low power consumption, wide coverage, robustness, flexibility, scalability and easy integration with industrial systems. In this paper, we present an efficient implementation scheme that enables monitoring and control of industrial processes through wireless sensors deployed inside the process area. We propose a novel approach called “Ultrasonic Lightweight Internet” (ULTIMATE) which combines ultrasonic radio frequency (RF) technologies with lightweight embedded system designs. The proposed system uses energy harvesting techniques to generate high power output from microcontrollers and other energy sources like solar cells or batteries. To communicate over the air, ultrasonic signals are transmitted using WSNs’ self-organizing topology called mesh network. This paper presents the overall architecture of the proposed system, its deployment conditions, performance evaluation, and future research directions.

**Keywords:** IoT, Wireless Sensor Network, Energy Harvesting
