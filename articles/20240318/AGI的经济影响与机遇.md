                 

AGI（People's Republic of China）
===================================

AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) is a subfield of AI (Artificial Intelligence) that focuses on creating machines capable of understanding, learning, and applying knowledge across a wide range of tasks at a level equal to or beyond human capability. This article explores the economic impact and opportunities of AGI.

1. Background Introduction
------------------------

### 1.1. The Evolution of AI

* Early AI (1950s-1980s): Symbolic reasoning, rule-based systems, and expert systems
* Machine Learning Era (1990s-present): Neural networks, deep learning, and reinforcement learning
* AGI: The next frontier in AI research, focused on creating machines with general intelligence

### 1.2. Definition and Scope of AGI

* AGI refers to artificial systems that can perform any intellectual task that a human being can do
* It involves understanding, learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language use
