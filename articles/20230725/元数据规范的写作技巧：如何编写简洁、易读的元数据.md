# 1.简介
         
随着互联网的蓬勃发展，用户对各种数据的获取越来越多，在数据的获取过程中产生了大量的数据元数据（metadata）。但是对于数据元数据的编写方式，目前存在很多不合理甚至错误的写法，给数据元数据分析、使用、共享造成了障碍。因此，本文旨在总结并阐述如何编写简洁、易读的元数据。文章主要包括两个方面内容：
1）元数据语法标准：对于元数据的语法结构要求，作者将按照ISO/IEC JTC1 SC29 WG11 NIST SP800-53：元数据管理应用框架（MDF）的定义进行制定；
2）元数据写作规范：基于ISO/IEC JTC1 SC29 WG11 NIST SP800-53 MDF的指导思想，总结并介绍元数据的写作规范，提高元数据的可读性和准确性。

# 2.元数据语法标准
## ISO/IEC JTC1 SC29 WG11 NIST SP800-53：元数据管理应用框架
ISO/IEC JTC1 SC29 WG11 NIST SP800-53:元数据管理应用框架 (MDF) 是由NIST Special Publication 800-53:信息安全管理原则和控制 (SP800-53) 中的信息分类、信息类型和信息收集、信息存储、信息流通和信息保护五个部分组成的控制框架。该框架的目的是通过统一、标准化的方法，以共同努力促进信息安全管理工作的顺利进行。

根据SP800-53的定义，元数据是关于某些事物的信息，用于描述、组织、储存或获得该事物的一组数据。它可以帮助人们更加容易地了解事物及其相关信息，从而实现信息的价值最大化。元数据通常包含重要的上下文信息、知识，也可能包含潜在的风险和安全隐患，需要受到严格的保护。

元数据管理应用框架(MDF)的核心部分包括：
- 元数据类别：描述信息的种类，如个人元数据、财务元数据、行为历史元数据等；
- 元数据属性：定义不同类别信息的特征和属性，如名字、出生日期、身份证号码等；
- 元数据结构：描述元数据的逻辑和数据结构，如键值对、树状结构、关系型数据库等；
- 元数据交换格式：定义元数据交换的格式，如XML、JSON、RDF等；
- 元数据管理工具：定义元数据的管理工具，如元数据仓库、搜索引擎、数据模型等；
- 元数据治理：对元数据实施治理手段，如合规性评估、分类管理、权限控制等。

为了编写符合SP800-53标准的元数据，通常需要遵循以下规则：
1. 数据来源准确且完整。每条元数据都应清晰记录数据采集、生成的来源，及其说明。
2. 属性准确。应该提供所有需要的信息，以便其他系统能够正确识别和处理元数据。
3. 元数据结构简单而直观。尽量减少冗余信息，使元数据可读性更佳。
4. 可扩展性强。采用开放式元数据交换格式，可以方便第三方服务集成。
5. 元数据生命周期短。通常只需要做一次元数据创建即可，不需要经过长时间的积累。
6. 不要遮掩重要的信息。对重要的个人或机密信息不要采用复杂的加密方案，而是采用透明的加密方式。

## 2.元数据写作规范
元数据一般分为三个层次，即数据层级、内容层级和形式层级。数据层级主要关注元数据的来源、采集、生成的时间、工具等，内容层级主要聚焦元数据的描述、用途、属性、约束条件等，形式层级侧重于元数据的发布、传输、接收、解析、使用等过程。

### 数据层级
#### 来源说明
数据来源通常出现在元数据文件的头部，用于描述元数据所属的数据的来源和起源。例如，数据来源可以包括原始数据文档的名称、创建者、发布日期、说明信息等。
```text
Data Source Name: Project Management Plan for ABC Corp
Creator: John Smith
Date Published: February 24, 2019
Version: v1.1
Description of Data: This document is a project management plan for the development and deployment of a new software system. It includes information on business objectives, scope, budgets, risks, roles and responsibilities, and timelines. The document also outlines the tools to be used in the process, as well as stakeholder feedback mechanisms and processes. Lastly, it describes how the project will be monitored, controlled, and managed over its lifetime.
``` 

#### 采集说明
采集人员负责收集数据并进行数据抽取、转换和加载。他们一般会将原始数据复制一份进行保存，以避免原始数据被修改或者损坏。同时，它们还要注意保护原始数据不被泄露、盗用、恶意利用、破坏等。

#### 生成说明
元数据生成的过程涉及元数据的创建、汇编、验证、合并、标准化、编码、序列化等步骤。其中，创建阶段负责收集数据源中的信息，汇编阶段根据数据来源和目的进行组合、整理、合并、排序，验证阶段检测和纠正数据错误，编码阶段将元数据转换为数字表示，最后序列化阶段将元数据输出成可读的文本文件。

### 内容层级
内容层级主要聚焦于元数据的属性、约束条件、限制、用途等细节。内容层级要求元数据具有完整、准确、详尽的信息。以下示例展示了元数据的描述、属性、约束条件、限制和用途等信息：
```text
Description of Dataset: This dataset contains employee records including personal data such as name, date of birth, and job title, as well as work history data such as start dates, end dates, positions held, departments worked at, and compensation received. These datasets are collected from various sources, ranging from company HR systems to third party databases containing employee data. They were originally created by employees themselves or underwritten by their employers to protect sensitive information about them. There may be duplicates or missing entries due to errors or omissions during data collection. Additional metadata attributes include version number, release date, and copyright information. To ensure privacy protection, no identifiable individuals or organizations can be linked directly to these datasets without permission from the owners. However, aggregated and anonymized versions of this dataset could still provide useful insights into demographics, organizational structure, and industry trends across different industries.

Attribute Information:
    Name:
        Unique identifier assigned to each individual employee who has been issued with an identity card or drivers license.

    Date of Birth:
        The date of birth entered as MMDDYYYY format.

    Job Title:
        The position that the individual holds within their organization.

    Start Date:
        The date when the individual started working for the organization.

    End Date:
        The date when the individual stopped working for the organization, if applicable.

    Position Held:
        The title or role of the individual's current position within the organization, if applicable.

    Department Worked At:
        The department where the individual works within the organization.
    
    Compensation Received:
        Annual salary, bonus, commission, or other earnings or benefits earned by the individual within the organization, if available.

Constraints:
    No direct links or references between any individual or entity identified in this dataset can be made without the explicit consent of the respective owner. Aggregated or de-identified versions of this dataset must not be shared or sold outside the organization without prior authorization from the owner. The use of these datasets for analysis purposes should be conducted solely within the bounds of compliance with all relevant laws, regulations, and policies. Personal information contained within these datasets must be kept confidential, secure, and protected from unauthorized access and disclosure.

Usage Guidelines:
    Use of this dataset requires appropriate safeguards against unintended disclosures and misuse of personal information. As part of the data governance strategy, owners of this dataset should establish procedures and protocols for ethical data usage, which include minimizing the risk of harm to individuals or organizations, maintaining security, and respecting human rights and fundamental freedoms. While public availability of this dataset provides valuable insights into the internal workings of an organization, it must never be relied upon as evidence of conflicts of interest, collusion, or otherwise unethical behavior. Individuals seeking further research beyond what is provided here should consult their own institutional memory or seek professional assistance from external sources.
