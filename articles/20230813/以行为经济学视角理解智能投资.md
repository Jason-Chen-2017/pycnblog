# 1.简介
  

为了帮助投资者更好地理解智能投资（AI investment），我们将从经济学、Behavioral Economics和Artificial Intelligence三个视角出发，进行一系列的阐述。首先，在介绍背景前，让我们先来了解一下什么是智能投资，它可以分为以下两种类型：

1) Behavioral-driven AI (BDAI) - 从人的行为习惯和动机出发，通过模仿人类的行为方式，利用机器学习算法来设计投资策略并生成量化交易信号；
2) Data-driven AI (DDIA) - 通过分析历史数据、深度学习等技术，建立模型预测未来股市走势，实现股票市场的精准预测和量化交易。

BDAI以人类行为为基础，通过系统atically design strategies for financial investing that mimic the human decision making process and generate quantitative trading signals using machine learning algorithms; DDIA uses historical data analysis and deep learning technologies to develop models that predict future stock market trends and provide a precise prediction of the stock market as well as a quantitative trading signal. 

那么，为什么要学习智能投资？我们都知道，人类的行为模式已经影响了全球金融市场的运行，比如股票市场、债券市场、基金市场等等，所以，如何根据个人的投资喜好、能力水平、经济状况以及环境来推荐最适合自己的理财产品也是个值得探讨的话题。

我们先来看一下，基于BDAI的智能投资，它的特点是由人的决策习惯驱动，并且在机器学习的指导下，通过学习行为习惯并运用强大的计算机模型，制定一套自己的投资策略，然后再根据投资回报评估系统，通过不断调整交易参数，形成一个投资组合。其主要工作流程如下所示：

1）系统atically design strategies for financial investing by imitating the behavior patterns of humans and applying machine learning techniques to optimize portfolio selection based on performance evaluation system

2）Use mathematical equations or machine learning models to create a portfolio optimization model that takes into account personal preferences, risk appetite, economic status, and environmental factors to recommend optimal portfolios

3）The portfolio optimization model is used alongside an automated trading algorithm that monitors the performance of each component of the portfolio over time and adjusts parameters accordingly in real-time.

基于数据驱动的AI(DDIA)的智能投资，通过利用大数据处理、分析、机器学习等技术，构建模型预测股市走势，并结合人工智能算法，优化个性化的投资策略。其主要工作流程如下所示：

1）Analyze and collect large amounts of historical data from various financial instruments such as equities, bonds, futures contracts, commodities, currencies etc., which can be used for generating insights and making predictions about future market trends.

2）Apply advanced machine learning algorithms like regression, clustering, neural networks, support vector machines, decision trees etc., to analyze and extract meaningful features from this data to train predictive models that can help us make accurate predictions about future market trends.

3）Develop customized algorithms to identify suitable investment opportunities within these predictions, based on users' preferences, risk tolerance, budget constraints, historic returns, current market conditions, available funds, liquidity needs etc.

4）Implement an automatic trading algorithm that continuously monitors user’s portfolio composition, selects best performing investment opportunities, optimizes their positions and executes trades in real-time, taking into consideration market prices, transaction costs, slippage losses and other factors affecting the overall profitability of the strategy.

最后，从人的行为经济学视角出发，既可以理解智能投资背后的经济学原理，又能看到智能投资的自身发展方向。
