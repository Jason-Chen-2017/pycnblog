## 一、背景介绍
“Policy Management”（策略管理），是指对企业管理模式进行调整，通过制定适合于企业特点和阶段的合理的策略，以协助企业实现长期目标。它涉及到企业的日常运作、管理机制、过程、流程、信息、人力资源、财务资源等各方面，其目标是确保企业运行顺利，满足客户的需求，具有可持续性。“Policy Management”与“Governance”（管理）相比，前者更注重具体执行过程，强调在各个环节落实政策，后者更注重企业的长远发展，强调国家和社会的管理意图。
“Policy Management”模式应用非常广泛，遍及各行各业，从商业机构到政府部门、金融机构等，用于向客户提供服务、产品、管理决策、财务报表等方面的改善与控制。“Policy Management”能够改善企业的竞争力、产品及服务的价值、员工的工作能力、社会的福祉等。
## 二、基本概念术语说明
### （一）Policies and Goals
Policies are rules or guidelines that specify what a company is expected to do in order to achieve its goals. For example, a corporate policy might state that the company has a minimum wage of $5 per hour and to reduce energy consumption by half each year. In IT management, policies typically focus on achieving business objectives such as improving customer satisfaction, reducing costs, increasing revenue, meeting service level targets, and managing risks to mission success.
Goals are long-term outcomes defined by the CEO or executive leadership team. These goals should be measurable and should have clear measurable criteria, such as reducing marketing spend or increasing sales revenue. They are often aligned with strategic directions established by the board of directors. Goals can help ensure that the company remains focused on delivering value and maintaining competitive advantage over rival companies.
