3.1 问题理解

$$ P(y|x) = \frac{e^{f(x,y)}}{\sum_{y'}e^{f(x,y')}} $$

3.2 知识检索

$$ sim(q, d) = \frac{q \cdot d}{\|q\| \|d\|} $$

3.3 答案生成

$$ P(y_{1:T}|x) = \prod_{t=1}^{T}P(y_t|y_{1:t-1}, x) $$
