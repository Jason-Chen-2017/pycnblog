# 2.核心概念与联系

2.1 GPS系统的组成

GPS系统由三个主要组成部分组成：卫星、接收器和控制中心。卫星是GPS系统的核心部分，它们 orbiting Earth at an altitude of approximately 20,200 kilometers (12,200 miles) and traveling at a speed of approximately 3.8 kilometers per second (2.4 miles per second). The satellites transmit signals that the GPS receiver uses to calculate its position. The control center is responsible for managing the satellites and ensuring that they are in the correct position.

2.2 GPS定位原理

GPS定位原理是基于时间和距离的三角定理。当接收器接收到来自卫星的信号时，它可以计算出自身与卫星之间的距离。通过计算多个卫星的距离，接收器可以计算出自身的位置。
