### 4.1 数学模型构建

### 4.2 公式推导过程

在详细推导公式之前，我们假定 $f$ 的表达式为 $f(z) = u(z) + iv(z)$，其中 $u$ 和 $v$ 分别是实部和虚部函数。由于 $f$ 是全纯的，我们有 Cauchy-Riemann 方程：

$$\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial v}{\partial y}, \quad \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = -\frac{\partial v}{\partial x}$$

对 $f$ 求偏导，我们得到：

$$\frac{\partial f}{\partial z} = \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} + i \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \right), \quad \frac{\partial f}{\partial \overline{z}} = \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} - i \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \right)$$

进一步计算偏导数的乘积：

$$\frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial z \partial \overline{z}} \cdot \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial \overline{z} \partial z} = \left( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} \frac{\partial v}{\partial y} \right)^2$$

利用 Cauchy-Riemann 方程，上式简化为：

$$\left( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} \frac{\partial v}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} \frac{\partial v}{\partial y} \right)^2 = \left( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} \frac{\partial u}{\partial x} \right)^2 = 0$$

因此，全纯截面曲率为零，意味着在这种情况下全纯截面本身并不提供额外的曲率信息。
