#### 1. 信贷风险评估
**题目：** 请描述如何使用大模型技术来进行信贷风险评估？

        self.short_window = 10
        self.long_window = 30
        self.short_moving_average = self.data.close.rolling(self.short_window).mean()
        self.long_moving_average = self.data.close.rolling(self.long_window).mean()

            self.buy()
        elif self.short_moving_average[-1] < self.long_moving_average[-1]:
            self.sell()
