### <u>4. 数学模型和公式 & 详细讲解 & 举例说明</u> (Mathematical Models and Formulas & Detailed Explanation & Examples)

#### 4.1 深度学习中的激活函数

激活函数是深度学习模型中的一个关键组件，它用于引入非线性因素，使得模型能够学习更复杂的函数。以下是一些常用的激活函数及其公式：

- **Sigmoid 函数**:
  $$ f(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-x}} $$

- **ReLU 函数**:
  $$ f(x) = \max(0, x) $$

- **Tanh 函数**:
  $$ f(x) = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} $$

- **Softmax 函数**:
  $$ f(x)_i = \frac{e^{x_i}}{\sum_{j} e^{x_j}} $$

#### 4.2 神经网络损失函数

损失函数用于评估模型预测值与真实值之间的差异，从而指导模型参数的调整。以下是一些常用的损失函数及其公式：

- **均方误差（MSE）**:
  $$ \text{MSE} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2 $$

- **交叉熵损失（Cross-Entropy Loss）**:
  $$ \text{Cross-Entropy} = -\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} y_i \log(\hat{y}_i) $$

#### 4.3 示例

假设我们有一个二分类问题，使用sigmoid激活函数和均方误差损失函数，我们可以得到以下模型：

$$
\hat{y} = \sigma(w_1 \cdot x_1 + w_2 \cdot x_2 + b)
$$

$$
\text{MSE} = \frac{1}{m} \sum_{i=1}^{m} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2
$$

其中，$w_1, w_2, b$ 是模型参数，$x_1, x_2$ 是输入特征，$y_i$ 是真实标签，$\hat{y}_i$ 是预测值。

在训练过程中，我们的目标是调整模型参数以最小化MSE损失函数。

**4. Mathematical Models and Formulas & Detailed Explanation & Examples**

**4.1 Activation Functions in Deep Learning**

Activation functions are a key component of deep learning models that introduce nonlinearity, enabling the model to learn more complex functions. Here are some commonly used activation functions and their formulas:

- **Sigmoid Function**:
  $$ f(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-x}} $$

- **ReLU Function**:
  $$ f(x) = \max(0, x) $$

- **Tanh Function**:
  $$ f(x) = \frac{e^x - e^{-x}}{e^x + e^{-x}} $$

- **Softmax Function**:
  $$ f(x)_i = \frac{e^{x_i}}{\sum_{j} e^{x_j}} $$

**4.2 Loss Functions in Neural Networks**

Loss functions evaluate the discrepancy between the model's predictions and the actual values, guiding the adjustment of model parameters. Here are some commonly used loss functions and their formulas:

- **Mean Squared Error (MSE)**:
  $$ \text{MSE} = \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2 $$

- **Cross-Entropy Loss**:
  $$ \text{Cross-Entropy} = -\frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} y_i \log(\hat{y}_i) $$

**4.3 Example**

Suppose we have a binary classification problem using the sigmoid activation function and mean squared error loss function. We can obtain the following model:

$$
\hat{y} = \sigma(w_1 \cdot x_1 + w_2 \cdot x_2 + b)
$$

$$
\text{MSE} = \frac{1}{m} \sum_{i=1}^{m} (y_i - \hat{y}_i)^2
$$

where $w_1, w_2, b$ are model parameters, $x_1, x_2$ are input features, $y_i$ is the actual label, and $\hat{y}_i$ is the predicted value.

The objective in the training process is to adjust the model parameters to minimize the MSE loss function.

#### 5.2 源代码详细实现

以下是一个简单的深度学习项目实例，用于预测患者的疾病风险。

```python
import tensorflow as tf
import numpy as np
import pandas as pd

# 读取数据
data = pd.read_csv('health_data.csv')
X = data.iloc[:, :-1].values
y = data.iloc[:, -1].values

# 数据预处理
X = X / 255.0
y = y.reshape(-1, 1)

# 创建模型
model = tf.keras.Sequential([
    tf.keras.layers.Dense(128, activation='relu', input_shape=(X.shape[1],)),
    tf.keras.layers.Dropout(0.2),
    tf.keras.layers.Dense(1, activation='sigmoid')
])

# 编译模型
model.compile(optimizer='adam',
              loss='binary_crossentropy',
              metrics=['accuracy'])

# 训练模型
model.fit(X, y, epochs=10, batch_size=32, validation_split=0.2)

# 评估模型
test_loss, test_acc = model.evaluate(X, y)
print(f"Test accuracy: {test_acc:.2f}")
```

- **评估模型**：使用测试数据评估模型性能。
