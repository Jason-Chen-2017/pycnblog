### 4.2 公式推导过程
大模型的数学公式推导过程较为复杂，涉及多层神经网络的正向传播和反向传播过程。具体推导过程如下：

$$
z_l = \sigma(W_l \cdot a_{l-1} + b_l)
$$

$$
a_l = \sigma(z_l)
$$

$$
\delta_l = \frac{\partial L}{\partial z_l} \cdot \frac{\partial z_l}{\partial a_l}
$$

$$
\frac{\partial z_l}{\partial a_{l-1}} = \frac{\partial z_l}{\partial z_{l-1}} \cdot \frac{\partial z_{l-1}}{\partial a_{l-1}}
$$

$$
\frac{\partial L}{\partial W_l} = a_{l-1}^T \cdot \delta_l
$$

$$
\frac{\partial L}{\partial b_l} = \delta_l
$$
