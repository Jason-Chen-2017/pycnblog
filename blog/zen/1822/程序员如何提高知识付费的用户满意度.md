    interest_degree = interest_counts.mean()

    clusters = behavior_analysis(scaled_data)
    interest_degree = identify_interest(clusters)
    print(f'User Interest Degree: {interest_degree}')

if __name__ == '__main__':

## 9. 附录：常见问题与解答

### 9.1 什么是知识付费？

**中文**：

知识付费是指用户为了获得特定知识或技能，付费购买相关课程、书籍、视频等学习资源。这种模式在互联网时代得以迅速发展，因为它满足了用户对高质量知识内容的迫切需求。

**English**：

Knowledge monetization refers to users purchasing learning resources such as courses, books, videos, etc., to gain specific knowledge or skills. This model has rapidly developed in the internet age as it meets the urgent demand for high-quality knowledge content from users.

### 9.2 提高用户满意度的关键因素是什么？

**中文**：

提高用户满意度的关键因素包括课程内容的专业性和实用性、个性化推荐、互动性体验、用户反馈机制以及平台的整体服务质量。这些因素共同决定了用户对知识付费平台的整体满意度。

**English**：

Key factors for enhancing user satisfaction include the professionalism and practicality of course content, personalized recommendations, interactive experiences, user feedback mechanisms, and the overall service quality of the platform. These factors together determine the overall satisfaction of users with the knowledge monetization platform.

### 9.3 如何确保知识付费内容的质量？

**中文**：

确保知识付费内容的质量可以从以下几个方面入手：

- **严格审核教师资质**：确保教师具有相关领域的专业背景和丰富的教学经验。
- **课程内容更新**：定期更新课程内容，确保其与行业趋势保持同步。
- **用户反馈**：积极收集用户反馈，对课程进行优化和改进。
- **第三方评估**：邀请第三方机构对课程进行质量评估，确保其符合行业标准。

**English**：

To ensure the quality of knowledge monetization content, the following measures can be taken:

- **Stringent Teacher Qualification Review**：Ensure that teachers have professional backgrounds and extensive teaching experience in their respective fields.
- **Course Content Updates**：Regularly update course content to ensure it remains aligned with industry trends.
- **User Feedback**：Actively collect user feedback to optimize and improve courses.
- **Third-Party Assessment**：Invite third-party organizations to assess course quality to ensure it meets industry standards.

### 9.4 如何提高用户互动体验？

**中文**：

提高用户互动体验可以通过以下方法实现：

- **在线讨论区**：建立在线讨论区，鼓励用户在学习过程中提问和交流。
- **直播互动**：定期举办直播课程，让用户与教师实时互动。
- **问答环节**：在课程结束后设置问答环节，解答用户疑问。
