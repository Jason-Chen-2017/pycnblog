具体操作步骤如下：

数学模型公式详细讲解：

- **边缘检测**：Canny算法

$$
G(x,y) = (G*h_x(x,y))^2 + (G*h_y(x,y))^2 - (G*h_x(x,y)*G*h_y(x,y))^2
$$

- **特征提取**：SIFT算法

$$
\begin{aligned}
\nabla_x I(x,y) &= I(x+\Delta x,y)-I(x-\Delta x,y) \\
\nabla_y I(x,y) &= I(x,y+\Delta y)-I(x,y-\Delta y)
\end{aligned}
$$

- **支持向量机**：最大稳定性解

$$
\begin{aligned}
\min_{w,b} \frac{1}{2}w^2 \\
s.t. \forall i, y_i(w^T x_i + b) \geq 1
\end{aligned}
$$

- **随机森林**：CART决策树

$$
\begin{aligned}
\min_{w,b} \frac{1}{2}w^2 \\
s.t. \forall i, y_i(w^T x_i + b) \geq 1
\end{aligned}
$$

- **卷积神经网络**：卷积层、池化层、全连接层

$$
\begin{aligned}
y = f(Wx + b)
\end{aligned}
$$
