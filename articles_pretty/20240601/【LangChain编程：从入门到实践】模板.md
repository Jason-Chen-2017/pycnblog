### 2.1. Blockchain Basics

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that records transactions in a secure and transparent manner. It consists of blocks, each containing a set of transactions, linked together using cryptographic techniques. The decentralized nature of blockchain ensures that no single entity has control over the network, making it highly resistant to tampering and fraud.

### 2.2. Natural Language Processing (NLP)

NLP is a subfield of AI that focuses on enabling computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language. NLP involves various techniques, such as text analysis, sentiment analysis, and machine translation, to process and understand human language.

### 2.3. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

AI refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn like humans. AI encompasses various subfields, including machine learning, deep learning, and robotics, and is used to develop intelligent systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as decision-making, problem-solving, and pattern recognition.

## 4. Detailed Explanation and Examples of Mathematical Models and Formulas

To create a LangChain program, developers need to understand the mathematical models and formulas used in NLP and AI.

### 4.1. NLP Models and Formulas

Some common NLP models and formulas include:

- **Bag of Words (BoW)**: A simple model that represents text as a bag of words, ignoring the order of words.
- **Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency (TF-IDF)**: A weighting scheme that reflects how important a word is to a document in a collection or corpus.
- **Word2Vec**: A deep learning model that learns word embeddings, representing words as vectors in a high-dimensional space.

### 4.2. AI Models and Formulas

Some common AI models and formulas include:

- **Support Vector Machines (SVM)**: A supervised learning algorithm that can be used for classification and regression tasks.
- **Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)**: A deep learning model that is particularly effective for image recognition tasks.
- **Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN)**: A deep learning model that is designed to handle sequential data, such as text.
