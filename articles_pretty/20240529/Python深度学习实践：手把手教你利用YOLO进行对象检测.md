### 4.2 损失函数
#### 4.2.1 位置损失
$$L_{loc} = \lambda_{coord}\sum_{i=0}^{S^2}\sum_{j=0}^{B}\mathbb{1}_{ij}^{obj}[(x_i-\hat{x}_i)^2+(y_i-\hat{y}_i)^2]$$
#### 4.2.2 置信度损失
$$L_{conf} = \sum_{i=0}^{S^2}\sum_{j=0}^{B}\mathbb{1}_{ij}^{obj}(C_i-\hat{C}_i)^2 + \lambda_{noobj}\sum_{i=0}^{S^2}\sum_{j=0}^{B}\mathbb{1}_{ij}^{noobj}(C_i-\hat{C}_i)^2$$
#### 4.2.3 类别损失
$$L_{class} = \sum_{i=0}^{S^2}\mathbb{1}_i^{obj}\sum_{c\in classes}(p_i(c)-\hat{p}_i(c))^2$$
