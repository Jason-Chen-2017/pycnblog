$$W(\mathbf{s}) = \sup_{0 \leq \alpha < \beta \leq 1} \left| \frac{1}{n} \sum_{i=1}^{n} \chi(\alpha s_i + \beta) - \int_0^1 \chi(\alpha x + \beta) dx \right|$$
