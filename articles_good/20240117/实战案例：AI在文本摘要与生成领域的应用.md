### 1.4.3 代码实例

```python
from transformers import pipeline

# 文本摘要示例
def summarize_text(text):
    summarizer = pipeline("summarization")
    summary = summarizer(text, max_length=130, min_length=30, do_sample=False)
    return summary[0]["summary_text"]

# 文本生成示例
def generate_text(prompt):
    generator = pipeline("text-generation")
    generated_text = generator(prompt, max_length=100, num_return_sequences=1, do_sample=False)
    return generated_text[0]["generated_text"]

# 测试文本摘要示例
text = "Elon Musk, the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, has announced that his companies will be working together on a new project. The project aims to develop a high-speed transportation system that will connect major cities across the United States. The system will use a series of tunnels and hyperloop technology to achieve speeds of up to 760 miles per hour. The project is expected to be completed within the next decade."

summary = summarize_text(text)
print(summary)

# 测试文本生成示例
prompt = "The European Space Agency (ESA) has announced a new mission to explore the Moon's south pole. The mission, called Lunar South Pole-Aitken Basin (LSPAB), will use a combination of robotic rovers and satellites to study the region's geology and resources. The mission is expected to launch in 2022 and will last for two years."

generated_text = generate_text(prompt)
print(generated_text)
```

在上述代码示例中，我们使用了Hugging Face的Transformers库，该库提供了多种预训练的模型，如BERT、GPT-2、GPT-3等，可以用于文本摘要与生成任务。通过简单的API调用，我们可以实现文本摘要与生成的功能。

## 1.1 背景介绍

文本摘要与生成是AI技术的一个重要应用之一，它可以帮助用户快速获取关键信息，提高信息处理效率。在新闻报道中，文本摘要可以帮助用户快速了解新闻内容，减少阅读时间。在搜索引擎中，文本生成可以帮助用户找到相关信息，提高搜索效率。在机器翻译中，文本摘要可以帮助用户快速了解翻译内容，提高翻译效率。
