\[ C = E \times F \times C_{\text{CO2}} \]

其中：
- \( E \) 是能源消耗量（单位：千瓦时，kWh）
- \( F \) 是能源转换效率（单位：%）
- \( C_{\text{CO2}} \) 是每单位能源的碳排放系数（单位：千克二氧化碳/千瓦时）

#### 2. 能源消耗量计算模型

能源消耗量（E）可以通过以下公式计算：

\[ E = P \times t \]

其中：
- \( P \) 是能源功率（单位：千瓦，kW）
- \( t \) 是能源使用时间（单位：小时，h）

#### 3. 能源利用效率计算模型

能源利用效率（\( \eta \)）可以通过以下公式计算：

\[ \eta = \frac{E_{\text{out}}}{E_{\text{in}}} \times 100\% \]

其中：
- \( E_{\text{out}} \) 是能源输出量（单位：千瓦时，kWh）
- \( E_{\text{in}} \) 是能源输入量（单位：千瓦时，kWh）

### Mathematical Models and Formulas & Detailed Explanation and Examples

To better understand the specific measures for achieving sustainability in Silicon Valley, we can introduce some mathematical models and formulas.

#### 1. Carbon Emission Calculation Model

Carbon emissions (C) can be calculated using the following formula:

\[ C = E \times F \times C_{\text{CO2}} \]

Where:
- \( E \) is energy consumption (unit: kilowatt-hours, kWh)
- \( F \) is energy conversion efficiency (unit: %)
- \( C_{\text{CO2}} \) is the carbon emission coefficient per unit of energy (unit: kilograms of carbon dioxide per kilowatt-hour)

#### 2. Energy Consumption Calculation Model

Energy consumption (E) can be calculated using the following formula:

\[ E = P \times t \]

Where:
- \( P \) is energy power (unit: kilowatts, kW)
- \( t \) is energy usage time (unit: hours, h)

#### 3. Energy Efficiency Calculation Model

Energy efficiency (\( \eta \)) can be calculated using the following formula:

\[ \eta = \frac{E_{\text{out}}}{E_{\text{in}}} \times 100\% \]

Where:
- \( E_{\text{out}} \) is energy output (unit: kilowatt-hours, kWh)
- \( E_{\text{in}} \) is energy input (unit: kilowatt-hours, kWh)

#### Detailed Explanation and Examples

To better understand the mathematical models and formulas, let's consider a specific example.

#### Example

Assume a technology company in Silicon Valley uses 100 kW of power, operates for 8000 hours per year, with an energy conversion efficiency of 30%, and a carbon emission coefficient of 0.4 kg CO2/kWh. We can calculate the annual carbon emissions of the company using the following formulas:

1. **Energy Consumption (E)**:

\[ E = P \times t = 100 \times 8000 = 800,000 \text{ kWh} \]

2. **Energy Efficiency (\( \eta \))**:

\[ \eta = \frac{E_{\text{out}}}{E_{\text{in}}} \times 100\% = \frac{0.3 \times E}{E} \times 100\% = 30\% \]

3. **Carbon Emissions (C)**:

\[ C = E \times F \times C_{\text{CO2}} = 800,000 \times 0.3 \times 0.4 = 96,000 \text{ kg CO2} \]

Therefore, the company's annual carbon emissions are 96,000 kg CO2.
