#### 4.1 生成对抗网络（GANs）

生成对抗网络（GANs）是一种基于博弈理论的机器学习框架，由生成器（Generator）和判别器（Discriminator）组成。生成器试图生成尽可能真实的广告内容，而判别器则试图区分真实广告内容和生成内容。

**数学模型：**

- **生成器（Generator）**：生成器的目标是生成逼真的广告内容。其数学模型可以表示为：

  $$ G(z) = x $$

  其中，$G(z)$ 是生成器生成的广告内容，$z$ 是随机噪声。

- **判别器（Discriminator）**：判别器的目标是判断输入的广告内容是否真实。其数学模型可以表示为：

  $$ D(x) = \begin{cases} 
  1, & \text{if } x \text{ is real} \\
  0, & \text{if } x \text{ is generated} 
  \end{cases} $$

  其中，$D(x)$ 是判别器对输入广告内容的判断结果。

**训练过程：**

GANs 的训练过程是一个动态博弈过程，生成器和判别器交替训练，不断优化。

1. **生成器训练**：生成器接收随机噪声 $z$，生成广告内容 $x$。生成器的损失函数可以表示为：

   $$ L_G = -\mathbb{E}_{z \sim p_z(z)}[\log D(G(z))] $$

2. **判别器训练**：判别器接收真实广告内容和生成广告内容，优化其判断能力。判别器的损失函数可以表示为：

   $$ L_D = -\mathbb{E}_{x \sim p_x(x)}[\log D(x)] - \mathbb{E}_{z \sim p_z(z)}[\log (1 - D(G(z))] $$

**实例说明：**

假设我们要使用 GANs 生成一张广告图片。首先，我们生成随机噪声 $z$，然后通过生成器 $G(z)$ 生成广告图片 $x$。接着，我们使用真实广告图片 $x'$ 和生成广告图片 $x$ 对判别器进行训练，通过迭代优化生成器和判别器，直到生成器能够生成逼真的广告图片。

#### 4.2 自然语言处理（NLP）

自然语言处理（NLP）中的模型如 GPT-3 和 BERT，用于生成高质量的广告文案。这些模型通过大量的文本数据学习语言的规律，从而能够生成符合语法和语义要求的广告文案。

**数学模型：**

- **GPT-3**：GPT-3 是一个基于 Transformer 的语言模型，其核心数学模型是一个自注意力机制（Self-Attention）。

  $$ \text{Attention}(Q, K, V) = \frac{QK^T}{\sqrt{d_k}}V $$

  其中，$Q$、$K$ 和 $V$ 分别是查询向量、键向量和值向量，$d_k$ 是键向量的维度。

- **BERT**：BERT 是一个基于 Transformer 的双向编码器，其核心数学模型包括自注意力机制和双向编码。

  $$ \text{Transformer}(X) = \text{LayerNorm}(X + \text{MultiHeadSelfAttention}(X)) + \text{LayerNorm}(X + \text{PositionWiseFeedForward}(X)) $$

  其中，$X$ 是输入的文本序列。

**实例说明：**

假设我们要生成一句广告文案。首先，我们将输入文本编码成向量表示，然后通过 GPT-3 或 BERT 模型进行自注意力计算和双向编码，得到句子的语义表示。最后，根据生成的语义表示，模型会输出一句符合语法和语义要求的广告文案。

#### 4.3 图像生成模型

图像生成模型如 StyleGAN 和 DALL-E，用于生成高质量的广告图像和视频。这些模型通过学习图像的特征，能够生成具有逼真外观的广告图像。

**数学模型：**

- **StyleGAN**：StyleGAN 是一种基于深度卷积神经网络的图像生成模型，其核心数学模型包括多层感知器和风格混合。

  $$ G(z) = \text{PerceptualNetwork}(z; \theta_g) + \text{StyleMix}(z; \theta_s) $$

  其中，$z$ 是输入的随机噪声，$\theta_g$ 和 $\theta_s$ 分别是生成器和风格混合的参数。

- **DALL-E**：DALL-E 是一种基于自注意力机制的图像生成模型，其核心数学模型是自注意力机制。

  $$ \text{DALL-E}(x) = \text{SelfAttention}(x) + \text{PositionalEncoding}(x) $$

  其中，$x$ 是输入的图像序列。

**实例说明：**

假设我们要生成一张广告图像。首先，我们生成随机噪声 $z$，然后通过 StyleGAN 或 DALL-E 模型生成广告图像 $x$。接着，我们对生成的图像进行后处理，如去噪、色彩调整等，以使其更加符合广告要求。

#### 4.1 Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)

Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) are a machine learning framework based on game theory, consisting of a generator and a discriminator. The generator aims to generate realistic advertising content, while the discriminator tries to distinguish between real advertising content and generated content.

**Mathematical Model:**

- **Generator**: The generator's goal is to produce realistic advertising content. Its mathematical model can be represented as:

  $$ G(z) = x $$

  Where $G(z)$ is the advertising content generated by the generator, and $z$ is the random noise.

- **Discriminator**: The discriminator's goal is to determine whether the input advertising content is real. Its mathematical model can be represented as:

  $$ D(x) = \begin{cases} 
  1, & \text{if } x \text{ is real} \\
  0, & \text{if } x \text{ is generated} 
  \end{cases} $$

  Where $D(x)$ is the discriminator's judgment result for the input advertising content.

**Training Process:**

The training process of GANs is a dynamic game process where the generator and discriminator alternate training, continuously optimizing.

1. **Generator Training**: The generator receives random noise $z$ and generates advertising content $x$. The loss function of the generator can be represented as:

   $$ L_G = -\mathbb{E}_{z \sim p_z(z)}[\log D(G(z))] $$

2. **Discriminator Training**: The discriminator receives real advertising content and generated advertising content to optimize its judgment ability. The loss function of the discriminator can be represented as:

   $$ L_D = -\mathbb{E}_{x \sim p_x(x)}[\log D(x)] - \mathbb{E}_{z \sim p_z(z)}[\log (1 - D(G(z))] $$

**Example Illustration:**

Assume we want to use GANs to generate an advertising image. First, we generate random noise $z$, then through the generator $G(z)$, we generate an advertising image $x$. Next, we use real advertising images $x'$ and generated advertising images $x$ to train the discriminator, iteratively optimizing the generator and discriminator until the generator can generate realistic advertising images.

#### 4.2 Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Natural Language Processing (NLP) models such as GPT-3 and BERT are used to generate high-quality advertising texts. These models learn the rules of language through large amounts of text data, enabling them to generate texts that comply with grammar and semantics.

**Mathematical Model:**

- **GPT-3**: GPT-3 is a language model based on the Transformer architecture, with the core mathematical model being the self-attention mechanism.

  $$ \text{Attention}(Q, K, V) = \frac{QK^T}{\sqrt{d_k}}V $$

  Where $Q$, $K$, and $V$ are the query vector, key vector, and value vector respectively, and $d_k$ is the dimension of the key vector.

- **BERT**: BERT is a bidirectional encoder based on the Transformer architecture, with the core mathematical model including the self-attention mechanism and bidirectional encoding.

  $$ \text{Transformer}(X) = \text{LayerNorm}(X + \text{MultiHeadSelfAttention}(X)) + \text{LayerNorm}(X + \text{PositionWiseFeedForward}(X)) $$

  Where $X$ is the input text sequence.

**Example Illustration:**

Assume we want to generate an advertising text. First, we encode the input text into a vector representation, then through the GPT-3 or BERT model, we perform self-attention calculation and bidirectional encoding to obtain the semantic representation of the sentence. Finally, based on the generated semantic representation, the model outputs a grammatically and semantically correct advertising text.

#### 4.3 Image Generation Models

Image generation models such as StyleGAN and DALL-E are used to generate high-quality advertising images and videos. These models learn image features and can generate advertising images with realistic appearances.

**Mathematical Model:**

- **StyleGAN**: StyleGAN is an image generation model based on deep convolutional neural networks, with the core mathematical model including multi-layer perceptrons and style mixing.

  $$ G(z) = \text{PerceptualNetwork}(z; \theta_g) + \text{StyleMix}(z; \theta_s) $$

  Where $z$ is the input random noise, and $\theta_g$ and $\theta_s$ are the parameters of the generator and style mixing respectively.

- **DALL-E**: DALL-E is an image generation model based on self-attention mechanisms, with the core mathematical model being the self-attention mechanism.

  $$ \text{DALL-E}(x) = \text{SelfAttention}(x) + \text{PositionalEncoding}(x) $$

  Where $x$ is the input image sequence.

**Example Illustration:**

Assume we want to generate an advertising image. First, we generate random noise $z$, then through the StyleGAN or DALL-E model, we generate an advertising image $x$. Next, we perform post-processing on the generated image, such as denoising and color adjustment, to make it more suitable for advertising requirements.
