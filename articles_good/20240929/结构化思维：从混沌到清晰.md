### 背景介绍

#### Background Introduction

#### 1. 结构化思维的定义

结构化思维是一种系统性的、有组织的思维方式，它有助于我们在面对复杂问题时，将问题分解为更小、更易管理的部分，并找到解决这些问题的有效方法。这种思维方式强调逻辑性、有序性和条理性，使得我们能够更清晰地理解问题，并制定出更有效的解决方案。

#### 1. Definition of Structured Thinking

Structured thinking is a systematic and organized way of thinking that helps us break down complex problems into smaller, more manageable parts and find effective methods to solve them. This approach emphasizes logic, orderliness, and systematicity, enabling us to understand problems more clearly and develop more effective solutions.

#### 2. 结构化思维的重要性

在现代社会，我们面临的问题越来越复杂，单一思维模式很难应对这些挑战。结构化思维可以帮助我们：

- **提高逻辑分析能力**：通过结构化思维，我们能够更好地理解问题的本质，找到问题之间的关联，从而提高我们的逻辑分析能力。
- **优化决策过程**：在决策过程中，结构化思维可以帮助我们系统地分析各种因素，避免因情绪或偏见导致的错误决策。
- **提升工作效率**：结构化思维使得我们在处理问题时更有条理，从而提高工作效率和绩效。

#### 2. The Importance of Structured Thinking

In today's modern society, the problems we face are increasingly complex, and a single思维方式 is often insufficient to address these challenges. Structured thinking can help us:

- **Enhance Logical Analysis Skills**: Through structured thinking, we can better understand the essence of problems, identify relationships between them, and thereby improve our logical analysis skills.
- **Optimize Decision-Making Process**: During the decision-making process, structured thinking enables us to systematically analyze various factors, thereby avoiding errors in decision-making due to emotions or biases.
- **Boost Work Efficiency**: Structured thinking allows us to approach problem-solving in an orderly manner, thereby enhancing our work efficiency and performance.

### 核心概念与联系

#### Core Concepts and Connections

#### 3. 结构化思维的组成部分

结构化思维主要包括以下几个组成部分：

- **问题识别**：首先，我们需要准确地识别问题，明确问题的性质和范围。
- **目标设定**：在识别问题后，我们需要设定一个明确的目标，以确保我们的解决方案能够解决问题。
- **信息收集**：为了制定有效的解决方案，我们需要收集与问题相关的信息。
- **方案评估**：在提出解决方案后，我们需要评估这些方案的可行性、效果和风险。

#### 3. Components of Structured Thinking

Structured thinking mainly consists of the following components:

- **Problem Identification**: Firstly, we need to accurately identify the problem and clarify its nature and scope.
- **Goal Setting**: After identifying the problem, we need to set a clear goal to ensure that our solutions address the issue.
- **Information Collection**: To develop an effective solution, we need to gather information related to the problem.
- **Solution Evaluation**: After proposing solutions, we need to evaluate their feasibility, effectiveness, and risks.

#### 4. 结构化思维与创造性思维的关系

结构化思维和创造性思维是相辅相成的。在解决问题时，我们需要既有条理地分析问题，又要跳出常规，寻找创新的解决方案。结构化思维为我们提供了一个有序的分析框架，而创造性思维则帮助我们打破思维定势，找到新颖的解决方案。

#### 4. The Relationship Between Structured Thinking and Creative Thinking

Structured thinking and creative thinking complement each other. When solving problems, we need to analyze them systematically while also breaking away from conventional thinking to find innovative solutions. Structured thinking provides us with an orderly analysis framework, while creative thinking helps us break away from mental patterns and find novel solutions.

#### 5. 如何运用结构化思维解决问题？

以下是一个运用结构化思维解决问题的具体步骤：

1. **问题识别**：首先，我们需要明确问题是什么，问题的性质和范围是什么。

2. **目标设定**：在明确问题后，我们需要设定一个清晰的目标，这个目标应该是具体、可衡量的。

3. **信息收集**：为了制定有效的解决方案，我们需要收集与问题相关的信息。

4. **方案评估**：在提出解决方案后，我们需要评估这些方案的可行性、效果和风险。

5. **方案实施**：选择最佳方案后，我们需要将其付诸实践。

6. **效果评估**：在实施解决方案后，我们需要评估其效果，并根据实际情况进行调整。

#### 5. How to Apply Structured Thinking to Solve Problems?

Here is a specific step-by-step approach to applying structured thinking to solve problems:

1. **Problem Identification**: Firstly, we need to clearly identify what the problem is and its nature and scope.
2. **Goal Setting**: After identifying the problem, we need to set a clear goal, which should be specific and measurable.
3. **Information Collection**: To develop an effective solution, we need to gather information related to the problem.
4. **Solution Evaluation**: After proposing solutions, we need to evaluate their feasibility, effectiveness, and risks.
5. **Solution Implementation**: Choose the best solution and put it into practice.
6. **Effectiveness Evaluation**: After implementing the solution, we need to evaluate its effectiveness and make adjustments as needed based on the situation.

import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

def preprocess_data(data):
    # 数据清洗和转换
    data = data.dropna()
    data['turnover_rate'] = data['离职率']

    data = pd.read_csv('员工流失数据.csv')

    # 数据预处理
    data = preprocess_data(data)

    # 数据可视化
    visualize_data(data)

if __name__ == '__main__':
    main()
