4. **数据分析**：

假设可穿戴设备收集到的睡眠数据为 $s_1, s_2, s_3, \dots, s_m$，则可以使用均值、中位数、方差等指标来分析睡眠质量：

$$
\mu_s = \frac{1}{m} \sum_{i=1}^{m} s_i
$$

$$
\text{Median}(s) = \left\{
\begin{array}{ll}
s_{\lceil \frac{m}{2} \rceil}, & \text{if } m \text{ is odd} \\
\frac{s_{\lceil \frac{m}{2} \rceil} + s_{\lfloor \frac{m}{2} \rfloor}}{2}, & \text{if } m \text{ is even}
\end{array}
\right.
$$

$$
\sigma_s^2 = \frac{1}{m-1} \sum_{i=1}^{m} (s_i - \mu_s)^2
